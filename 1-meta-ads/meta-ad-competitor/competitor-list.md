## What to Extract From Each Competitor

When reviewing ads from anyone on this list, capture:

1. **Hook type** (pain point, metric, question, bold statement, story)
2. **Opening line** (first 125 characters before "see more")
3. **Creative format** (image, video, carousel, UGC)
4. **CTA style** (soft vs hard, what action they drive)
5. **Offer positioning** (how they frame the value)
6. **Emotional trigger** (fear, aspiration, curiosity, social proof)
7. **Run duration** (longer running = higher performing)
