## 🎯 HOW TO USE THIS SWIPE FILE

### Step 1: Match to Your Objective
- **Awareness:** Statement, Story, Question
- **Consideration:** Pain Point, How-To, Authority
- **Conversion:** Metric/Result, Bold Statement, Urgency

### Step 2: Adapt to Your Voice
- Keep Ben's British English
- Add his direct, Yorkshire tone
- Remove AI jargon
- Use his natural phrases

### Step 3: Test 3-5 Variations
- Don't just copy one
- Combine patterns (Metric + Question)
- A/B test different hooks
- Track which converts

---

## 📋 CHECKLIST BEFORE POSTING

✅ Hook grabs attention in first 125 characters
✅ Clear benefit (what's in it for them?)
✅ Sounds like a human, not AI
✅ British English spelling
✅ One sentence per line (if multi-line)
✅ CTA matches stage (awareness = no CTA, conversion = hard CTA)
✅ No forbidden AI phrases ("Here's the thing", "Let's dive in")
