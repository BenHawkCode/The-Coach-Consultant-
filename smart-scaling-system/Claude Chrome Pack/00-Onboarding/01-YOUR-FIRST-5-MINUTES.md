# Your First 5 Minutes

## Step 1: Install Claude Chrome (30 seconds)

Go to the Chrome Web Store and search "Claude" by Anthropic. Click "Add to Chrome". Done.

You'll see the Claude icon appear in your browser toolbar (top right). That's it. No account setup beyond your existing Claude account.

## Step 2: Open a Competitor's Website (30 seconds)

Think of one competitor in your space. Go to their homepage right now.

## Step 3: Open Claude Chrome (10 seconds)

Click the Claude icon in your toolbar. A chat panel opens on the right side of your screen. Claude can now see everything on the page you're looking at.

## Step 4: Paste This Prompt (10 seconds)

Copy and paste this into Claude Chrome:

---

**COPY THIS:**

```
Analyse this entire page and give me a complete competitor intelligence report.

Break it down into:

1. POSITIONING - What are they claiming to be? Who are they targeting? What language are they using to describe their audience?

2. OFFER STRUCTURE - What are they selling? How is it packaged? What pricing can you see or infer?

3. SOCIAL PROOF - What testimonials, logos, case studies, or trust signals are on this page?

4. MESSAGING STRENGTHS - What are they doing well in their copy? What hooks or phrases stand out?

5. MESSAGING WEAKNESSES - Where is the copy vague, generic, or unconvincing? What's missing?

6. CALLS TO ACTION - What CTAs are on this page? How are they positioned? What's the primary conversion goal?

7. WHAT I CAN STEAL - Give me 5 specific things I could adapt for my own business in [YOUR NICHE].

Be specific. Use exact quotes from the page where relevant.
```

---

## Step 5: Read the Output (3 minutes)

Claude will analyse the entire page and give you a structured intelligence report. No scraping tools. No paid software. No tech skills.

You just got a competitor analysis that would take a VA 2-3 hours, done in under 60 seconds.
