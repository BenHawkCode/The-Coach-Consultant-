# Prompt 57: Amazon Book Review Miner

**Category:** Market Research & Audience Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Amazon book reviews page
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Mines Amazon book reviews in your niche to understand what your audience loved, what disappointed them, and what was missing entirely. Extracts language patterns, content gaps, and product ideas from readers who have already spent money to solve the problem you address.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Amazon Book Review Mining

Analyse the reviews for this book with a focus on audience intelligence for [YOUR NICHE].

## Extract the following:

### What Readers Loved
- What specific chapters, concepts, or frameworks get praised most?
- What "aha moments" do reviewers describe?
- What outcomes did readers achieve after applying the book's advice?
- Pull exact quotes that describe the value they received

### What Disappointed Readers
- What did readers expect that the book failed to deliver?
- Which sections were described as weak, vague, or unhelpful?
- What complaints appear in 2-3 star reviews specifically?
- Pull exact quotes that describe their frustrations

### What Was Missing
- What topics did reviewers wish the book had covered?
- What follow-up questions are readers left with?
- What "next step" are readers looking for after finishing?
- These gaps are your content and product opportunities

### Language Patterns
- How do readers describe their problem BEFORE reading the book?
- How do they describe the transformation AFTER applying the advice?
- What emotional words appear most frequently (frustrated, overwhelmed, stuck, confident, clear)?
- Compile a before/after language bank for [YOUR BRAND]'s marketing

### Audience Profile
- Based on the reviews, describe who is buying this book
- What level are they at (beginner, intermediate, advanced)?
- What other solutions have they tried?
- What is their primary motivation for buying?

### Content & Product Gaps
- Identify 5 content pieces that address what the book missed
- Identify 2 product or service ideas that fill the gaps reviewers describe
- Suggest how [YOUR BRAND] could position [YOUR OFFER] as the practical next step

Focus on patterns across multiple reviews, not individual opinions.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A clear picture of what your audience values most when learning about your topic
- Specific gaps and disappointments you can address with your own content and offers
- A before/after language bank for writing copy that resonates emotionally
- An audience profile based on people who have already spent money in your niche
- Content ideas that fill the exact gaps readers are frustrated about
- Product positioning opportunities based on unmet reader needs

## Pro Tips
- Mine reviews for the top 3 books in your niche to find patterns that repeat - those are the universal pain points
- The "What Was Missing" section is the most valuable - these are proven content topics with built-in demand
- 3-star reviews contain the most useful feedback because readers explain exactly what worked and what didn't
