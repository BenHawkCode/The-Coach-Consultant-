# Prompt 59: Competitor Directory Scraper

**Category:** Market Research & Audience Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Any business directory listing
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Scrapes a business directory listing to extract competitor positioning, service descriptions, pricing signals, and review sentiment. Identifies how competitors present themselves and where you can differentiate your offer to stand out.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Competitor Directory Intelligence

Analyse this business directory listing for competitive intelligence relevant to [YOUR NICHE].

## Extract the following:

### Business Profile
- Company name, location, and years in business if listed
- Primary category and subcategories they're listed under
- Website URL and social media links if visible
- Any certifications, awards, or badges displayed

### Positioning Analysis
- How does this business describe itself in its headline or tagline?
- What is their primary value proposition based on the listing?
- Who is their target audience based on the language used?
- What tone do they use - corporate, casual, technical, aspirational?

### Service & Offer Breakdown
- List every service or product mentioned in the listing
- Note any pricing information, packages, or tiers visible
- Identify their lead offer (the service they push most prominently)
- Flag any services that overlap with [YOUR OFFER]

### Review & Reputation Analysis
- Overall rating and total number of reviews
- Summarise what positive reviewers praise most
- Summarise what negative reviewers complain about
- Note any patterns in review responses from the business

### Differentiation Opportunities
- What is this competitor doing well that [YOUR BRAND] should match or exceed?
- What are they doing poorly that [YOUR BRAND] can exploit?
- What are they NOT saying or offering that represents a gap?
- How could [YOUR BRAND] position against this competitor specifically?

### Listing Optimisation Insights
- What keywords does this listing use effectively?
- What sections of the listing are strong vs weak?
- If [YOUR BRAND] has a listing on this directory, what should you improve based on this competitor's approach?
- Suggest an optimised tagline for [YOUR BRAND] that positions against this competitor

Output as a competitive brief. Keep it factual and actionable.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A complete breakdown of how your competitor positions themselves publicly
- Service and pricing intelligence for competitive benchmarking
- Review patterns showing where they're strong and where they're vulnerable
- Specific differentiation opportunities for your brand and offer
- Listing optimisation insights if you share the same directory
- A competitive brief you can reference when updating your own positioning

## Pro Tips
- Run this on 5-10 competitors in the same directory to build a full competitive landscape map
- Pay attention to the categories competitors list under - these reveal the keywords they're targeting
- Use the differentiation opportunities to update your own directory listing, website copy, and sales materials
