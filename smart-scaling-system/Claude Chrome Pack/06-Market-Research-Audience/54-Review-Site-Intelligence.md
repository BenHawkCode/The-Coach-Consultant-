# Prompt 54: Review Site Intelligence

**Category:** Market Research & Audience Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Trustpilot/G2/Google reviews page
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Mines customer reviews for business intelligence - what people praise, what they complain about, what needs aren't being met, and the exact language they use. Turns competitor reviews into your positioning strategy, content ideas, and sales copy ammunition.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Review Site Intelligence Mining

Analyse the reviews on this page for business intelligence relevant to [YOUR NICHE].

## Extract the following:

### Praise Patterns
- What do positive reviewers consistently mention?
- Which specific features, outcomes, or experiences get the most praise?
- Pull exact phrases from 5-star reviews that describe the value received
- What emotional language do satisfied customers use?

### Complaint Patterns
- What do negative reviewers consistently complain about?
- Group complaints into categories (e.g., pricing, support, results, experience)
- Note which complaints appear across multiple reviews - these are systemic issues
- Pull exact phrases from 1-2 star reviews that describe frustrations

### Unmet Needs
- What are reviewers asking for that this product/service doesn't deliver?
- What workarounds are customers describing?
- What would make a "good" experience "great" according to reviewers?

### Competitor Weaknesses
- Based on negative reviews, list the top 5 vulnerabilities of this business
- For each weakness, describe how [YOUR BRAND] could position against it
- Identify promises this business makes that it fails to deliver on

### Language Bank
- Compile 10 phrases from reviews that describe the problem (before buying)
- Compile 10 phrases that describe the desired outcome (what they wanted)
- Compile 10 phrases that describe disappointment (unmet expectations)
- These are ready-to-use for [YOUR BRAND]'s ad copy and sales pages

### Content Opportunities
- Based on the gaps above, suggest 5 content pieces that address common concerns
- Each piece should position [YOUR BRAND] as the better alternative

Format with direct quotes. Prioritise patterns over individual outlier reviews.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A clear map of what customers value most and what frustrates them
- Competitor vulnerabilities you can position your offer against directly
- A language bank of real customer phrases for your sales copy and ads
- Unmet needs that represent product or service opportunities for your business
- Content ideas that address the exact concerns your audience already has
- Emotional triggers you can ethically use in your marketing

## Pro Tips
- Run this on your top 3 competitors' review pages to build a comprehensive competitive intelligence file
- The "Language Bank" section is gold for Meta ad copy - use their exact words in your primary text
- Pay special attention to 3-star reviews - these contain the most balanced and detailed feedback
