# Prompt 53: Google Trends Analyser

**Category:** Market Research & Audience Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Google Trends results page
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Analyses Google Trends data for your niche to identify seasonal patterns, rising topics, declining interest areas, and regional differences. Gives you specific content timing recommendations so you publish the right topics at the right time.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Google Trends Analysis

Analyse the Google Trends data visible on this page for [YOUR NICHE].

## Break down the following:

### Trend Overview
- What is the overall direction of interest? (rising, stable, declining)
- Identify any significant spikes or drops and note the approximate dates
- Compare the current interest level to the peak - what percentage of peak are we at?

### Seasonal Patterns
- Identify any repeating patterns across years (monthly, quarterly, seasonal)
- Note which months consistently show highest interest
- Note which months consistently show lowest interest
- Recommend the best months to push content and offers based on these patterns

### Rising & Related Topics
- List all rising topics and queries shown
- For each rising topic, explain why it matters for [YOUR NICHE]
- Identify which rising topics represent content opportunities for [YOUR BRAND]
- Flag any "breakout" topics - these need immediate attention

### Declining Areas
- Identify any related topics or queries showing declining interest
- Note what this decline signals about audience behaviour shifts
- Recommend whether to avoid these topics or reframe them

### Regional Differences
- Note any geographic concentration of interest if visible
- Identify regions with highest and lowest interest
- Recommend whether to tailor content or offers by region

### Content Timing Recommendations
- Based on all the above, provide a month-by-month content focus plan
- Identify the optimal window to launch offers or campaigns in [YOUR NICHE]
- Flag any upcoming seasonal spikes to prepare content for now

Be specific with dates and percentages where visible on the page.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A clear picture of whether interest in your niche is growing, stable, or declining
- Seasonal patterns so you can time your content and launches for maximum impact
- Rising topics to create content about before your competitors catch on
- A month-by-month content timing plan based on real search demand
- Regional insights for targeting if you run paid ads or location-specific offers

## Pro Tips
- Compare your main topic against 2-3 related terms to see which framing resonates most with searchers
- Set the date range to 5 years to spot reliable seasonal patterns versus one-off spikes
- Check rising queries monthly - these often become mainstream 3-6 months later
