# Prompt 60: Trending Topic Aggregator

**Category:** Market Research & Audience Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Google Trends or X/Twitter trending
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Aggregates trending topics and filters them for relevance to your niche. Identifies which trends are worth creating content about, suggests specific angles to cover, and flags time-sensitive opportunities that need fast action.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Trending Topic Aggregation

Analyse the trending topics visible on this page and filter for relevance to [YOUR NICHE].

## Extract the following:

### Relevant Trends
- List every trending topic that connects to [YOUR NICHE] - directly or indirectly
- For each, explain the connection in one sentence
- Rate relevance: high (directly about your niche), medium (adjacent topic you can tie in), low (loose connection)
- Ignore anything with zero relevance - do not force connections

### Trend Context
- For each relevant trend, summarise what is driving the conversation
- Is this a news event, cultural moment, seasonal topic, or viral content?
- How long is this trend likely to last? (hours, days, weeks)
- Is the sentiment mostly positive, negative, or divided?

### Content Angles
- For each high-relevance trend, suggest 2 specific content pieces for [YOUR BRAND]
- Include a working title and the platform it suits best (Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, email)
- Frame each angle to position [YOUR BRAND] as an authority, not a trend-chaser
- Suggest a hook for each piece that ties the trend to [YOUR AUDIENCE]'s problems or goals

### Time-Sensitive Opportunities
- Flag any trends that need content published within 24-48 hours to be relevant
- For these, provide a quick-publish format: a caption or short post [YOUR BRAND] could publish today
- Note which trends will still be relevant next week (evergreen angles on trending topics)

### Platform-Specific Notes
- Are certain trends stronger on specific platforms?
- Where is the conversation happening most actively?
- Which platform should [YOUR BRAND] prioritise for each trend?

### Trends to Avoid
- Flag any trending topics that are controversial, political, or risky for [YOUR BRAND] to comment on
- Note any trends that seem popular but have no genuine connection to [YOUR NICHE]
- Explain why each should be avoided

Be ruthless with relevance. Five strong connections are worth more than fifteen weak ones.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A filtered list of trends that actually matter to your niche, ranked by relevance
- Context on what's driving each trend and how long it's likely to last
- Ready-to-use content angles with titles and platform recommendations
- Time-sensitive opportunities with quick-publish formats for same-day content
- A clear list of what to avoid so you don't waste time on irrelevant trends
- Platform-specific guidance on where each conversation is strongest

## Pro Tips
- Check trending topics at the same time each day for a week to spot patterns in when your niche trends peak
- The "evergreen angles on trending topics" are the most valuable - they let you ride the trend without being dependent on timing
- Batch this with your weekly content planning so trending topics inform your calendar rather than interrupt it
