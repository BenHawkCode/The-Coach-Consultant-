# Prompt 55: Industry Report Summariser

**Category:** Market Research & Audience Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Any industry report or PDF in browser
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Summarises a long industry report into actionable insights tailored to your niche. Pulls out the key statistics, identifies trends that directly affect your business, and translates dry data into specific opportunities and content you can act on immediately.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Industry Report Summary

Summarise this industry report with a focus on actionable intelligence for [YOUR NICHE].

## Extract the following:

### Key Statistics
- List the 10 most important data points from this report
- For each stat, explain in one sentence why it matters for [YOUR NICHE]
- Flag any statistics that would work well as content hooks or social proof

### Trends Affecting My Niche
- Identify every trend mentioned that impacts [YOUR NICHE] directly or indirectly
- For each trend, rate it as: opportunity, threat, or both
- Note the timeframe - is this happening now, 6 months out, or 12+ months away?

### Market Opportunities
- Based on the data, identify 3 specific opportunities for [YOUR BRAND]
- For each opportunity, describe the gap in the market it represents
- Suggest how [YOUR OFFER] could be positioned to capitalise on each one

### Threats & Risks
- What warning signs does this report contain for businesses in [YOUR NICHE]?
- What shifts in buyer behaviour should [YOUR BRAND] prepare for?
- What could make your current positioning less effective?

### Actionable Takeaways
- List 5 specific actions [YOUR BRAND] should take based on this report
- Prioritise by impact: which action would move the needle most?
- Include a timeline for each action (this week, this month, this quarter)

### Content Ammunition
- Pull 5 statistics or findings that would make strong social media posts
- Suggest a headline angle for each one
- Note which stats would work as authority-building content for [YOUR BRAND]

Keep the summary concise. I want insights, not a rewrite of the report.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- The 10 most important statistics with context for why each matters to your business
- A clear breakdown of which trends are opportunities and which are threats
- Specific actions to take this week, this month, and this quarter
- Ready-to-post content angles backed by real data and statistics
- A strategic view of where your market is heading so you can position ahead of it

## Pro Tips
- Save the "Content Ammunition" section and schedule one stat-based post per week for authority building
- Cross-reference findings with your Google Trends data to validate whether the trends are showing up in search behaviour
- Run this on 2-3 reports from different sources to see which findings are consistent - those are the trends you can bet on
