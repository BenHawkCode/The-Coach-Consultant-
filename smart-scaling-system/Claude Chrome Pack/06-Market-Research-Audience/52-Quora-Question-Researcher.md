# Prompt 52: Quora Question Researcher

**Category:** Market Research & Audience Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Quora topic or question page
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Extracts and organises the questions your audience is actively asking on Quora. Groups them by theme, identifies high-intent questions that signal buying readiness, and generates content angles and authority-building opportunities from each one.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Quora Question Research

Analyse this Quora page for audience intelligence relevant to [YOUR NICHE].

## Extract and organise:

### Question Inventory
- List every question visible on this page
- For each question, note the number of answers and followers if visible
- Flag questions with high engagement (many answers or followers)

### Theme Grouping
- Group all questions into 3-5 core themes
- Name each theme clearly (e.g., "Pricing concerns", "Getting started", "Choosing between options")
- Note which theme has the most questions - this is your audience's primary concern

### High-Intent Questions
- Identify questions where the asker is clearly close to making a purchase or hiring decision
- Pull out questions that contain words like "best", "worth it", "should I", "how much", "recommend"
- These are your highest-value content and ad targeting opportunities

### Content Angles
- For each theme, suggest 2 specific content pieces (blog post, video, carousel) with working titles
- Frame each piece to position [YOUR BRAND] as the authority answer
- Note which content format suits each question type best

### Authority-Building Opportunities
- Identify 3 questions where a detailed, expert answer would build credibility for [YOUR BRAND]
- For each, outline the key points your answer should cover
- Note any existing answers that are weak, generic, or outdated - these are easy wins

Keep all output actionable. No filler, just opportunities.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A full inventory of questions your audience is asking, grouped by theme
- High-intent questions that signal buying readiness you can target with content or ads
- Ready-to-use content angles with working titles for each theme
- Specific authority-building opportunities where you can position yourself as the expert
- A clear picture of which topics your audience cares about most

## Pro Tips
- Focus on questions with lots of followers but few quality answers - these are underserved opportunities
- Use the exact question phrasing as YouTube video titles or blog post headlines for SEO
- Answer 2-3 high-intent questions directly on Quora with a soft CTA back to your content
