# Prompt 58: Podcast Episode Summariser

**Category:** Market Research & Audience Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Podcast episode page with description
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Summarises a podcast episode page to extract key topics, guest insights, and actionable takeaways. Identifies content repurposing angles from the episode and flags outreach opportunities with the host or guest for collaboration.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Podcast Episode Summary & Intelligence

Analyse this podcast episode page for insights relevant to [YOUR NICHE].

## Extract the following:

### Episode Overview
- Summarise the core topic in 2-3 sentences
- Who is the guest and what is their area of expertise?
- What is the host's angle or perspective on the topic?
- What audience is this episode aimed at?

### Key Topics Covered
- List every distinct topic or subtopic discussed (based on the description, timestamps, and show notes)
- For each topic, write a one-sentence summary of the likely position or insight shared
- Flag any topics that are directly relevant to [YOUR NICHE]

### Actionable Takeaways
- Identify 3-5 specific pieces of advice or strategies mentioned
- For each, note whether it applies to [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- Flag any frameworks, models, or step-by-step processes referenced

### Content Repurposing Angles
- Suggest 3 social media posts inspired by topics from this episode
- Suggest 1 long-form content piece (blog, video, or email) that builds on the discussion
- Identify any contrarian or unique angles worth responding to with [YOUR BRAND]'s perspective

### Outreach Opportunities
- Would this host be a good fit for [YOUR BRAND] to pitch as a guest?
- What topic could you pitch that complements but doesn't duplicate this episode?
- If the guest is relevant, suggest a connection angle for reaching out
- Note any links to the host's or guest's social profiles if visible

### Competitive Intelligence
- What positioning does the guest or host take in [YOUR NICHE]?
- How does their approach differ from [YOUR BRAND]'s?
- Is there anything they're saying that you should be addressing in your own content?

Keep the summary tight. Focus on what's useful, skip what's generic.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A concise summary of the episode's core topics and guest expertise
- Actionable takeaways you can apply or reference in your own content
- Ready-to-adapt content repurposing angles for social media and long-form
- Guest pitching opportunities with a suggested topic angle
- Competitive intelligence on how others in your niche are positioning themselves
- Connection and outreach angles for networking with hosts and guests

## Pro Tips
- Use the "Content Repurposing Angles" to create response content - agreeing, disagreeing, or expanding on points made in the episode
- Track podcast guests in your niche across multiple shows to identify who the key voices are and where collaboration opportunities exist
- If you spot a gap in what the episode covers, pitch yourself as a guest to fill that gap on the same show
