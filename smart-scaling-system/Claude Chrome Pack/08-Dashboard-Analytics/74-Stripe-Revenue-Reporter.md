# Prompt 74: Stripe Revenue Reporter

**Category:** Dashboard & Analytics Reading
**Where to Use:** Stripe dashboard
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Translates your Stripe dashboard into a proper business revenue report. Instead of raw transaction data, you get MRR calculations, churn signals, product performance, and clear indicators of whether your revenue is healthy, growing, or needs attention.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Stripe Dashboard - Business Revenue Report

You are reading my Stripe dashboard. Translate everything visible into a business revenue report I can act on.

## Break down the following:

### Revenue Summary
- Total revenue for the visible period
- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) if subscriptions are visible
- One-time vs recurring revenue split
- Revenue trend - is it growing, flat, or declining?

### Churn Signals
- Any failed payments or declined cards visible?
- Subscription cancellations or downgrades in the period
- What percentage of revenue is at risk from churn indicators?
- Recommended actions to recover failed payments

### Top Products
- Which products or price points generate the most revenue?
- Which have the highest volume of transactions?
- Any products underperforming that need attention?

### Payment Trends
- Average transaction value
- Payment method breakdown if visible
- Any patterns in when payments come through (weekly, monthly cycles)?

### Revenue Health Score
- Rate my revenue health out of 10 based on what you can see
- Top 3 strengths in my revenue position
- Top 3 risks or concerns

### Growth Opportunities
- Where is the easiest revenue growth based on this data?
- What would I need to do to increase MRR by 20%?
- Any pricing or packaging changes the data suggests?

My business is [YOUR BRAND] and my main offer is [YOUR OFFER].
Give me the numbers straight - no sugar-coating.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Clear revenue summary with MRR and growth trend analysis
- Early warning churn signals with recovery actions
- Product performance ranking by revenue contribution
- Payment pattern insights and transaction analysis
- Honest revenue health score with strengths and risks
- Specific growth opportunities backed by the data

## Pro Tips
- Run this on the first of each month to build a revenue tracking habit
- Pay close attention to the churn signals section - catching failed payments early recovers 30-40% of at-risk revenue
- Compare your report month-on-month to spot trends before they become problems
