# Prompt 76: Google Search Console Interpreter

**Category:** Dashboard & Analytics Reading
**Where to Use:** Google Search Console dashboard
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Interprets your Google Search Console data and turns it into a practical SEO action plan. Identifies which queries you're ranking for, where you're close to page one, which pages need attention, and gives you specific quick wins you can implement this week.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Google Search Console - SEO Action Plan

You are reading my Google Search Console dashboard. Interpret the data and give me a practical SEO action plan focused on quick wins.

## Analyse the following:

### Top Queries
- Which search terms are driving the most impressions?
- Which search terms are driving the most clicks?
- What's the gap between impressions and clicks? (CTR opportunities)
- Any surprising queries I'm ranking for that I could capitalise on?

### CTR Opportunities
- Which queries have high impressions but low CTR?
- What position are these ranking at?
- Specific title tag and meta description improvements to boost CTR
- Which pages could jump significantly with small improvements?

### Position Improvements
- Which queries have moved up in position recently?
- Which queries have dropped - and should I be concerned?
- Queries ranking positions 5-15 that are closest to page one
- What would it take to push these onto page one?

### Pages Needing Attention
- Which pages get impressions but no clicks?
- Which pages have declining performance?
- Any pages with technical issues flagged?
- Priority order for fixing these pages

### Quick SEO Wins
- Top 5 actions I can take this week to improve organic traffic
- Rank these by effort vs. impact
- For each action, tell me exactly what to change and on which page

My website is [YOUR WEBSITE] and my niche is [YOUR NICHE].
Focus on actions I can take this week, not long-term projects.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Ranked list of your top performing and underperforming queries
- CTR improvement opportunities with specific title/meta suggestions
- Position tracking with alerts for drops and near-page-one opportunities
- Page-level audit with priority fixes
- Top 5 quick-win actions ranked by effort vs. impact
- Specific changes to make on specific pages this week

## Pro Tips
- Set the date range to 28 days for actionable data, 3 months for trend spotting
- Queries ranking positions 8-15 are your biggest opportunity - they're close to page one with the least effort
- Run this fortnightly and track whether your quick wins actually moved the needle
