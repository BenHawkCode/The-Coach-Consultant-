# Prompt 71: Google Analytics Translator

**Category:** Dashboard & Analytics Reading
**Where to Use:** Google Analytics dashboard
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Translates your Google Analytics data into plain English so you actually know what's working. Instead of staring at graphs and guessing, you get a clear breakdown of your top pages, where your traffic comes from, how visitors behave, and what to do next.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Google Analytics Plain English Translation

You are reading my Google Analytics dashboard. Translate everything visible into a clear, jargon-free business report.

## Break down the following:

### Traffic Overview
- Total sessions, users, and pageviews for the visible period
- Compare to previous period if shown - are things trending up or down?

### Top Pages
- Which pages get the most traffic?
- Which pages have the highest engagement (time on page, low bounce rate)?
- Which pages are underperforming despite getting traffic?

### Traffic Sources
- Where are visitors coming from? (organic, social, direct, referral, paid)
- Which source delivers the highest quality traffic (lowest bounce, longest session)?

### User Behaviour
- What's the typical journey through my site?
- Where are people dropping off?
- What conversion paths exist from entry to [YOUR OFFER]?

### What's Working
- Top 3 things performing well right now

### What Needs Fixing
- Top 3 issues to address this week
- Specific actions I can take for each

My niche is [YOUR NICHE] and my website is [YOUR WEBSITE].
Write everything in plain English - no analytics jargon.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Plain English summary of all visible traffic data
- Ranked list of your best and worst performing pages
- Traffic source breakdown with quality indicators
- Clear user behaviour patterns and drop-off points
- Prioritised list of what's working and what needs attention
- Specific actions to improve weak areas this week

## Pro Tips
- Run this on the last 28 days view for the most useful snapshot
- Compare month-on-month by switching the date range and running again
- Pair with Prompt 80 (Weekly Metrics Snapshot) to build a recurring reporting habit
