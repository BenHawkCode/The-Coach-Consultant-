# Prompt 78: YouTube Studio Analyser

**Category:** Dashboard & Analytics Reading
**Where to Use:** YouTube Studio analytics page
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Analyses your YouTube Studio stats and gives you a clear content strategy based on what the data actually says. Covers watch time, CTR, retention, subscriber growth, and tells you exactly what type of content to make more of and what to change.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# YouTube Studio Analytics - Content Strategy Report

You are reading my YouTube Studio analytics page. Analyse everything visible and give me a data-backed content strategy.

## Analyse the following:

### Watch Time Patterns
- Total watch time for the visible period
- Which videos have the highest total watch time?
- Average view duration across videos
- Which videos keep people watching longest (highest percentage watched)?
- What do the high-retention videos have in common?

### CTR by Video
- Which thumbnails/titles are getting the highest click-through rate?
- Which videos have high impressions but low CTR? (thumbnail/title problem)
- What patterns exist in the top-performing thumbnails/titles?
- Specific improvements for low-CTR videos

### Audience Retention Insights
- Where do viewers typically drop off in my videos?
- Do I have a hook problem (drop-off in first 30 seconds)?
- Do I have a pacing problem (gradual decline through the middle)?
- Which videos have the flattest retention curve and why?

### Subscriber Growth
- New subscribers for the period and trend direction
- Which videos drove the most subscriptions?
- Subscriber-to-view ratio - are viewers converting?
- What content type converts viewers to subscribers best?

### Content Strategy Recommendations
- Top 3 video topics/formats I should make more of based on the data
- Top 3 things to change about my current approach
- Ideal video length based on retention data
- Thumbnail and title patterns to replicate
- One specific video idea based on what's performing best

My niche is [YOUR NICHE] and my audience is [YOUR AUDIENCE].
Base every recommendation on the data - not general YouTube advice.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Watch time analysis identifying your highest-performing content
- CTR breakdown with specific thumbnail and title improvements
- Audience retention diagnosis (hook, pacing, or structure issues)
- Subscriber conversion analysis showing what drives growth
- Data-backed content strategy with specific topics and formats
- One ready-to-film video idea based on proven performance

## Pro Tips
- Check the "Audience" tab before running this - knowing when your viewers are online shapes your upload schedule
- Videos with high impressions but low CTR are your biggest opportunity - the algorithm is showing them, people just aren't clicking
- Run this monthly and track whether your content strategy changes are improving retention over time
