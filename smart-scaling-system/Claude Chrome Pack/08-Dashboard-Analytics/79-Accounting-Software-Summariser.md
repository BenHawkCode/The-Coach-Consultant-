# Prompt 79: Accounting Software Summariser

**Category:** Dashboard & Analytics Reading
**Where to Use:** Xero/QuickBooks/FreeAgent dashboard
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Summarises your accounting dashboard into a plain English financial health report. Covers profit and loss, cash flow, expenses, income trends, and flags anything that needs your attention - without requiring you to understand accounting terminology.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Accounting Dashboard - Financial Health Summary

You are reading my [Xero/QuickBooks/FreeAgent] dashboard. Summarise everything visible into a plain English financial health report.

## Break down the following:

### Profit & Loss Overview
- Total income for the visible period
- Total expenses for the visible period
- Net profit/loss and the margin percentage
- How does this compare to previous period if shown?
- Is the business actually making money after all costs?

### Cash Flow Status
- Current cash position (money in the bank)
- Money coming in vs. money going out this period
- Any upcoming large payments or invoices due?
- How many months of runway at current burn rate?
- Cash flow trend - improving or tightening?

### Expense Categories
- Top 5 expense categories by amount
- Any categories that have increased significantly?
- Which expenses are essential vs. could be reduced?
- Where am I potentially overspending?

### Income Trends
- Revenue sources breakdown if visible
- Which income streams are growing?
- Which are declining or inconsistent?
- Concentration risk - am I too dependent on one source?

### Financial Health Flags
- Green flags: what looks healthy
- Amber flags: things to watch over the next 30-60 days
- Red flags: anything that needs immediate attention
- Top 3 financial actions for this month

My business is [YOUR BRAND] in the [YOUR NICHE] space.
Write this for a business owner, not an accountant. Plain English only.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Clear profit and loss summary with margin analysis
- Cash flow assessment with runway calculation
- Expense breakdown with reduction opportunities
- Income trend analysis with concentration risk check
- Traffic light financial health flags (green, amber, red)
- Top 3 prioritised financial actions for the month

## Pro Tips
- Run this at the end of each month before your accountant meeting so you actually understand the numbers
- Pay special attention to the cash flow section - profitable businesses still fail from poor cash flow
- If any red flags appear, address them before spending on growth activities
