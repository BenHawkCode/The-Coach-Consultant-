# Prompt 73: CRM Pipeline Advisor

**Category:** Dashboard & Analytics Reading
**Where to Use:** Your CRM pipeline view
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Analyses your CRM pipeline and tells you exactly where deals are getting stuck, which opportunities are going stale, and what specific actions to take on each deal. Turns a messy pipeline view into a prioritised action list so you close more and lose fewer.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# CRM Pipeline Analysis & Action Plan

You are reading my CRM pipeline view. Analyse every visible deal and give me a clear action plan to maximise revenue from what's in front of me.

## Analyse the following:

### Pipeline Overview
- Total number of deals and total pipeline value
- Breakdown by stage - how many deals and what value at each stage?
- Is the pipeline shape healthy? (wide at top, narrow at bottom)

### Bottlenecks
- Which stage has the most deals stuck?
- How long have deals been sitting at each stage on average?
- What's causing the blockage at the slowest stage?

### Stale Opportunities
- Which deals haven't been touched or moved in 7+ days?
- Which of these are worth saving vs. should be closed-lost?
- Specific re-engagement action for each worth saving

### Close Rate Indicators
- Based on the visible data, what's my likely close rate?
- Which deals are closest to closing and what do they need?
- Which deals are least likely to close and why?

### Revenue Forecast
- Realistic revenue from this pipeline in the next 30 days
- Best case vs. worst case estimate
- What needs to happen to hit best case?

### Deal-by-Deal Actions
- For each visible deal, give me ONE specific next action
- Prioritise by likelihood to close and deal value

My offer is [YOUR OFFER] at the price point shown in the pipeline.
Be direct - tell me what I'm doing wrong and what to fix.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Full pipeline health assessment with stage-by-stage breakdown
- Identification of exactly where and why deals are getting stuck
- Stale deal list with save-or-kill recommendations
- Realistic 30-day revenue forecast with best/worst case
- Prioritised action list for every deal in your pipeline
- Specific bottleneck fixes to improve your overall close rate

## Pro Tips
- Run this weekly to catch stale deals before they go completely cold
- If a deal has been in the same stage for more than 14 days without activity, it's probably dead - be honest with yourself
- Use the deal-by-deal actions as your Monday morning task list
