# Prompt 72: Social Media Insights Reader

**Category:** Dashboard & Analytics Reading
**Where to Use:** Instagram/LinkedIn insights page
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Reads your social media insights and turns raw numbers into a clear content strategy. You get specific advice on when to post, what content types perform best, who your audience actually is, and exactly what to do more of (and less of) based on real data.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Social Media Insights - Actionable Breakdown

You are reading my [Instagram/LinkedIn] insights dashboard. Analyse everything visible and give me a practical content strategy based on the data.

## Analyse the following:

### Best Posting Times
- When does my audience engage most?
- Which days of the week perform best?
- Give me a specific posting schedule based on the data

### Top Content Types
- Which format gets the most reach? (carousels, reels, static, stories, text posts)
- Which format gets the most engagement (comments, shares, saves)?
- Which format drives the most profile visits or link clicks?

### Audience Demographics
- Who is actually following me? (age, location, gender split)
- Does this match my ideal audience of [YOUR AUDIENCE]?
- Any surprises or mismatches to address?

### Engagement Trends
- Is engagement going up, down, or flat over the visible period?
- What content caused any spikes or dips?
- What's my average engagement rate and is it healthy for my size?

### Growth Recommendations
- Top 3 content types I should double down on
- Top 3 things I should stop doing or change
- One specific content idea based on what's performing best

My niche is [YOUR NICHE] and my brand is [YOUR BRAND].
Be specific with numbers - don't just say "good" or "bad".
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Data-backed posting schedule tailored to your audience
- Ranked list of your best performing content formats
- Audience demographic breakdown with match analysis
- Engagement trend summary with cause-and-effect insights
- Specific content recommendations based on real performance data
- Clear stop/start/continue list for your content strategy

## Pro Tips
- Run this on both Instagram and LinkedIn separately - what works on one rarely works identically on the other
- Check insights on a 30-day view minimum so you have enough data to spot real patterns
- Screenshot your insights before running this prompt so you have a record to compare next month
