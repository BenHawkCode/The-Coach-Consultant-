# Prompt 75: GoHighLevel Campaign Analyser

**Category:** Dashboard & Analytics Reading
**Where to Use:** GHL campaign/email stats page
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Analyses your GoHighLevel campaign stats and tells you exactly what's working, what's underperforming, and how to optimise. Covers open rates, click rates, reply rates, sequence performance, and gives you specific copy and timing recommendations to improve results.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# GoHighLevel Campaign Performance Analysis

You are reading my GoHighLevel campaign or email stats page. Analyse everything visible and give me a full performance breakdown with optimisation recommendations.

## Analyse the following:

### Email Performance
- Open rates for each email or campaign visible
- Click rates and which links/CTAs are getting clicks
- Reply rates if visible
- Unsubscribe rates and any deliverability concerns
- How do these compare to industry benchmarks for [YOUR NICHE]?

### Sequence Performance
- Which emails in the sequence perform best?
- Where in the sequence do people drop off?
- Is there a clear "winner" email that outperforms the rest?
- What's the overall sequence conversion rate?

### A/B Test Results
- If any A/B tests are visible, which variant won and by how much?
- What does the winning variant tell us about what the audience responds to?
- Recommendations for next A/B tests based on what we've learned

### Subject Line Analysis
- Which subject lines got the highest open rates?
- What patterns exist in the top performers? (length, tone, curiosity, urgency)
- Give me 3 new subject line ideas based on what's working

### Optimisation Recommendations
- Top 3 changes to make right now to improve performance
- Any emails that should be rewritten or removed from the sequence
- Send time recommendations based on engagement patterns
- Segment recommendations if audience data is visible

My audience is [YOUR AUDIENCE] and my offer is [YOUR OFFER].
Be specific - tell me exact changes, not vague suggestions.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Full performance breakdown of every visible campaign and email
- Sequence drop-off analysis showing where you lose people
- A/B test interpretation with next test recommendations
- Subject line pattern analysis with 3 new ideas to test
- Prioritised optimisation list with specific changes to make
- Benchmark comparison so you know if your numbers are actually good

## Pro Tips
- Export your GHL data before running this for the most complete analysis
- Focus on reply rates, not just open rates - replies indicate genuine interest
- Run this after every campaign send, not just monthly - small tweaks compound fast
