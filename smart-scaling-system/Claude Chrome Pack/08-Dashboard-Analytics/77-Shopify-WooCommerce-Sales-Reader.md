# Prompt 77: Shopify/WooCommerce Sales Reader

**Category:** Dashboard & Analytics Reading
**Where to Use:** Shopify or WooCommerce dashboard
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Reads your ecommerce dashboard and translates sales data into clear business decisions. Covers sales trends, product performance, cart abandonment signals, customer value indicators, and gives you specific revenue actions you can take immediately.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Ecommerce Dashboard - Sales & Revenue Analysis

You are reading my [Shopify/WooCommerce] dashboard. Analyse everything visible and give me a clear revenue action plan.

## Analyse the following:

### Sales Trends
- Total revenue for the visible period
- Average order value (AOV)
- Number of orders and how this compares to previous periods
- Revenue trend - growing, flat, or declining?
- Any seasonal patterns or spikes visible?

### Product Performance
- Which products generate the most revenue?
- Which products sell the most units?
- Which products have the highest margin (if data visible)?
- Any products with declining sales that need attention?
- Opportunities for bundling or upselling based on what sells together

### Cart Abandonment Signals
- Checkout conversion rate if visible
- Where in the purchase flow are people dropping off?
- What might be causing abandonment? (price, shipping, trust)
- Specific actions to recover abandoned carts

### Customer Lifetime Value Indicators
- Returning customer rate vs. new customers
- Average purchases per customer if visible
- Which products drive repeat purchases?
- What could increase customer retention?

### Revenue Actions
- Top 3 things to do this week to increase sales
- One pricing or bundling change to test
- One marketing action the data supports
- What to stop doing based on underperforming areas

My brand is [YOUR BRAND] and my audience is [YOUR AUDIENCE].
Give me actions, not just observations.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Sales trend analysis with period-over-period comparison
- Product performance ranking with bundle and upsell opportunities
- Cart abandonment diagnosis with specific recovery actions
- Customer retention analysis with lifetime value indicators
- Prioritised revenue action plan for the coming week
- Specific pricing and marketing recommendations backed by data

## Pro Tips
- Run this on a 30-day view for tactical actions, 90-day view for strategic decisions
- Pay close attention to the returning customer rate - acquiring a new customer costs 5x more than retaining one
- If your AOV is flat, the product bundling recommendations are usually the fastest way to increase it
