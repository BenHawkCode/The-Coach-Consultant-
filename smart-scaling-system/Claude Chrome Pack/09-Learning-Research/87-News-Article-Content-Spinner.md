# Prompt 87: News Article Content Spinner

**Category:** Learning & Research
**Where to Use:** Any news article
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Turns a trending news article into multiple content angles for your brand. Instead of just sharing the link, you get opinion takes, audience tie-ins, social hooks, email topics, and thought leadership angles - all tailored to your niche so you can ride the wave while it's relevant.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# News Article Content Spinner

Read this news article and turn it into content angles for my brand.

## My Context
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- My brand voice: [e.g. direct and no-nonsense / educational and supportive / bold and provocative]
- My platforms: [e.g. Instagram, LinkedIn, email, YouTube]

## Generate These Content Angles
1. Hot take - my bold opinion on this news that positions me as a thought leader
2. Audience tie-in - how this news directly affects my audience and what they should do
3. 3 social media hooks (scroll-stopping first lines) I can use for posts about this
4. Email subject line and opening paragraph that uses this as a hook
5. Contrarian angle - the perspective most people in my niche aren't talking about
6. Educational breakdown - teaching my audience what this means for them
7. Conversation starter - a question I can ask my audience to drive engagement
8. Content series potential - could this topic sustain 3-5 pieces of content

Make every angle specific to [YOUR NICHE]. No generic commentary.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A bold opinion take that positions you as someone worth listening to
- An audience-specific angle showing your followers you understand their world
- Ready-to-use social media hooks you can post within the hour
- An email angle with subject line and opening paragraph drafted
- A contrarian perspective that separates you from everyone sharing the same take
- Assessment of whether this topic has legs for a content series

## Pro Tips
- Speed matters with news content - run this prompt the same day the article drops and post within hours, not days
- Use the contrarian angle when everyone in your niche is saying the same thing - that's where you stand out
- Save the hooks in a swipe file even if you don't post immediately - they work as templates for future news stories
