# Prompt 86: Conference & Event Scout

**Category:** Learning & Research
**Where to Use:** Event/conference webpage
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Scouts a conference or event page and extracts everything worth knowing before you commit your time and money. Identifies the speakers worth following, sessions relevant to your niche, networking opportunities, and content angles you can create from the event - whether you attend or not.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Conference & Event Scout

Analyse this event page and tell me whether it's worth my time and investment.

## My Context
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My business goal right now: [e.g. lead generation, brand visibility, partnerships, learning]
- My audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- My brand: [YOUR BRAND]
- Budget consideration: [e.g. happy to invest / need strong ROI justification]

## What I Need
1. Event overview - what's the theme, who's it aimed at, what level
2. Speakers worth following - who has relevant expertise for my niche
3. Sessions directly relevant to my business goals - ranked by priority
4. Networking opportunities - who's likely in the room, any VIP access
5. Content angles I can create from this event (posts, stories, recaps)
6. Potential business leads or partnership opportunities
7. Whether this event is worth attending in person vs following online
8. 3 people I should try to connect with at this event and why

Give me a clear attend/skip/follow-online recommendation.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A quick overview of what the event is actually about beyond the marketing
- A ranked list of speakers and sessions worth your time
- Networking intelligence on who's likely attending and how to connect
- Content ideas you can create before, during, and after the event
- A clear recommendation on whether to attend, skip, or follow remotely

## Pro Tips
- Run this on events you're NOT attending too - you can still create content around the themes and tag speakers for visibility
- Use the speaker list to find people worth following on LinkedIn and Instagram regardless of whether you go
- If you do attend, use the content angles list to plan your posts in advance so you're not scrambling on the day
