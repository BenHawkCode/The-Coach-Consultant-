# Prompt 88: YouTube Video Notes Taker

**Category:** Learning & Research
**Where to Use:** YouTube video page
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Takes structured notes from a YouTube video page using the title, description, timestamps, and comments. Extracts the key points, maps action items to your business, and generates content ideas inspired by the video - so you get maximum value without rewatching or taking manual notes.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# YouTube Video Notes Taker

Analyse this YouTube video page and create structured notes for me.

## My Context
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- Why I'm watching this: [e.g. learning, competitor research, content inspiration, skill building]

## What I Need
1. Video overview - topic, creator credibility, intended audience
2. Key points extracted from the title, description, and any visible timestamps
3. Timestamp mapping - which sections are most relevant to my business
4. Top insights from the comments - what resonated most with viewers
5. Action items I can take from this video for my own business
6. Content ideas this video inspires for my brand and audience
7. Whether this creator is worth subscribing to and why
8. One thing I can implement today based on what this video covers

Focus on what's actionable for a business owner in [YOUR NICHE], not just a summary.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A structured breakdown of the video's core content and creator context
- Key points mapped to the timestamps so you can skip to what matters
- The most valuable insights from the comments section
- Specific action items tailored to your business
- Content ideas you can create inspired by (not copying) the video
- A clear recommendation on whether the creator is worth following

## Pro Tips
- Use this on competitor videos to understand what content is resonating with your shared audience
- Run it on tutorial videos to create a personal knowledge base of actionable notes you can search later
- The comments section analysis is gold - it tells you exactly what problems your audience cares about most
