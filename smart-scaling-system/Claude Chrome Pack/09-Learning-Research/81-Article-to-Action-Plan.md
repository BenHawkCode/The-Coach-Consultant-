# Prompt 81: Article to Action Plan

**Category:** Learning & Research
**Where to Use:** Any article or blog post
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Converts any article you're reading into a structured action plan tailored to your business. Instead of bookmarking articles you'll never revisit, you extract the key insights immediately and map them to specific actions you can take this week.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Article to Action Plan

Read this article and convert it into an actionable business plan for me.

## My Context
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My offer: [YOUR OFFER]
- My audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- Current business stage: [e.g. £50K revenue, scaling to £100K]

## What I Need
1. Extract the 3-5 key insights from this article - skip the filler
2. For each insight, explain how it specifically applies to my business
3. Rate each insight: immediate action / worth testing / background knowledge
4. Create a prioritised action list - what to do first, second, third
5. Build a 7-day implementation timeline with specific daily tasks
6. Flag anything that contradicts what's already working in [YOUR NICHE]

Keep it practical. No theory without a clear next step.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A distilled list of the article's core insights stripped of padding
- Each insight mapped directly to your business context
- A priority rating so you know what matters most right now
- A day-by-day implementation timeline you can follow immediately
- Flags for anything that might not apply to your specific situation

## Pro Tips
- Use this on long-form articles (2,000+ words) where the signal-to-noise ratio is low - that's where it saves the most time
- Run it on competitor blog posts to reverse-engineer their strategy and find gaps you can exploit
- Stack multiple article action plans together at the end of the week to spot recurring themes worth doubling down on
