# Prompt 91: Article to Instagram Carousel

**Category:** Content Creation from the Web
**Where to Use:** Any article or blog post
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Transforms any article you're reading into a complete 10-slide Instagram carousel with hook, value slides, and CTA. Saves hours of content creation by extracting the best ideas from existing content and reformatting them for Instagram.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Turn This Article Into a 10-Slide Instagram Carousel

Read this article and create a complete Instagram carousel for my brand.

## My Details
- Niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- Brand: [YOUR BRAND]
- Target audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]

## Carousel Structure
- Slide 1: Hook slide - bold, curiosity-driven headline that stops the scroll
- Slides 2-9: One key point per slide - short, punchy, actionable
- Slide 10: CTA slide tied to [YOUR OFFER]

## Rules
- Extract the 8 strongest points from the article
- Rewrite everything in my brand voice - not a copy of the original
- Each slide: max 3 short sentences or a single powerful statement
- Use pattern interrupts (questions, stats, bold claims) across slides
- No fluff, no filler, no "let's dive in" nonsense

## Also Provide
- A caption (under 150 words) with a strong opening line
- 3 hashtag sets (10 hashtags each): niche, growth, engagement
- A suggested CTA for the caption
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A complete 10-slide carousel with hook, 8 value slides, and CTA
- Each slide written in short, punchy format ready for design
- A caption with strong opening line and call to action
- Three sets of hashtags tailored to your niche
- Content rewritten in your voice - not just copied from the source

## Pro Tips
- Run this on articles your competitors wrote to create better versions of their content
- Save carousels as templates - swap the topic but keep the structure
- Use the hashtag sets on rotation across posts for maximum reach
