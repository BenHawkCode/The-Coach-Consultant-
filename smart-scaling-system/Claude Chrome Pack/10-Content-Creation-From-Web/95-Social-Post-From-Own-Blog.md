# Prompt 95: Social Posts from Your Own Blog

**Category:** Content Creation from the Web
**Where to Use:** Your published blog post
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Takes one of your own published blog posts and turns it into 5 different social media posts - each with a different format and angle. One blog post becomes a week of social content. Stops you from posting the same "new blog post" link and hoping for the best.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Turn My Blog Post Into 5 Social Media Posts

Read my blog post and create 5 different social posts to promote it across platforms.

## My Details
- Brand: [YOUR BRAND]
- Audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- Offer: [YOUR OFFER]
- Blog URL: [YOUR WEBSITE]

## Create These 5 Post Formats

### Post 1: Key Quote Pull
- Extract the single most powerful sentence or insight from the post
- Frame it as a standalone statement that makes people stop scrolling
- Add 1-2 lines of context beneath it

### Post 2: Hot Take
- Take a point from the post and turn it into a bold, slightly controversial opinion
- Lead with the take, then back it up with reasoning from the article

### Post 3: Question Post
- Turn the blog's main insight into a question that sparks discussion
- Follow with a short teaser that hints at the answer without giving it away

### Post 4: Listicle
- Pull 3-5 actionable tips from the post and format as a numbered list
- Each point: one sentence max

### Post 5: Story Format
- Turn one example or case study from the post into a short narrative
- Before/after or problem/solution structure
- End with the lesson learned

## Rules
- Each post must work standalone - no "check out my new blog post" energy
- Write in first person, conversational tone
- Include a soft CTA on each that links back to the full post
- Optimise for engagement, not just clicks
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Five completely different social posts from one blog article
- A key quote pull that works as a standalone authority post
- A hot take that drives comments and debate
- A question post designed to boost engagement
- A listicle for quick-value sharing
- A story post that builds connection and trust

## Pro Tips
- Schedule these across Monday to Friday so one blog post fuels a full week
- Test which format gets the most engagement and double down on that style
- Repurpose the listicle as a carousel and the story as a Reel script for even more mileage
