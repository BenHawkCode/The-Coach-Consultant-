# Prompt 92: Website Page to Marketing Email

**Category:** Content Creation from the Web
**Where to Use:** Any content page
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Takes any content page you're browsing - a blog post, landing page, or resource page - and converts it into a marketing email for your list. Extracts the core message, rewrites it for email format, and adds your personal angle so it reads like you wrote it from scratch.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Turn This Page Into a Marketing Email

Read this page and create a marketing email based on the core message.

## My Details
- Brand: [YOUR BRAND]
- Audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- What I sell: [YOUR OFFER]
- My website: [YOUR WEBSITE]

## Email Structure
1. Subject line (under 50 characters, curiosity-driven)
2. Preview text (under 90 characters)
3. Opening line that hooks - personal, conversational, direct
4. The core insight from this page rewritten in my voice
5. A personal angle - why this matters to me and my audience
6. Soft CTA - no hard sell, guide them to the next step
7. Sign-off that feels human

## Rules
- Write like I'm emailing one person, not a list
- Short paragraphs - 1-2 sentences max per paragraph
- No corporate language, no jargon, no filler
- The email should feel like it came from experience, not from reading an article
- Total length: 150-250 words
- CTA links to [YOUR WEBSITE] or [YOUR OFFER]
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A complete email with subject line and preview text
- Conversational copy that sounds like you, not a newsletter template
- The core message distilled and rewritten for your audience
- A personal angle that connects the content to your experience
- A soft CTA that drives action without being pushy

## Pro Tips
- Run this on 5 different pages in one sitting to batch a week of emails
- Save the best-performing subject line formats and feed them back as examples
- Use your own blog posts too - repurposing your content into email keeps your list warm
