# Prompt 96: Testimonial Page to Social Post

**Category:** Content Creation from the Web
**Where to Use:** Client testimonial page
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Takes a client testimonial page and turns it into a social media post that sells without selling. Extracts the transformation, builds a narrative caption around it, writes a hook that grabs attention, and adds a CTA. Turns social proof into scroll-stopping content.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Turn This Testimonial Into a Social Media Post

Read this testimonial page and create a social post that showcases the result.

## My Details
- Brand: [YOUR BRAND]
- Audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- What I sell: [YOUR OFFER]
- Niche: [YOUR NICHE]

## Extract From the Testimonial
1. The specific transformation (before vs after)
2. The key result or metric mentioned
3. The emotional shift (how they felt before vs after)
4. Any specific quote that hits hard

## Build the Post

### Hook (first line)
- Lead with the result, not the person
- Make it specific and outcome-driven
- Example format: "[Specific result] in [timeframe]" or "From [before state] to [after state]"

### Caption Body
- Tell the story in 3 beats: where they were, what changed, where they are now
- Keep it under 120 words
- Write it as a narrative, not a testimonial dump
- Weave in what made the difference without being salesy

### CTA
- Soft close - "If you're in the same spot, link in bio" or a question
- No hard sell, no urgency tactics

## Also Provide
- An alternative hook option
- A suggested image/visual direction for the post
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A complete social post with hook, narrative caption, and CTA
- The transformation distilled into a compelling before/after story
- An alternative hook to test different angles
- A visual direction suggestion for the accompanying image
- Copy that sells through storytelling, not pitching

## Pro Tips
- Batch 5-10 testimonials in one session to build a month of social proof content
- Pair each post with a screenshot of the original testimonial for credibility
- Rotate between result-led hooks and emotion-led hooks to keep your feed varied
