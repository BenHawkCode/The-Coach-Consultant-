# Prompt 98: Statistics Page to Authority Post

**Category:** Content Creation from the Web
**Where to Use:** Any statistics or data page
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Takes any page with statistics, research data, or industry reports and turns it into an authority social post. Extracts the most relevant stats for your audience, builds a narrative around them, adds your perspective, and writes a hook that makes people stop and pay attention.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Turn These Statistics Into an Authority Social Post

Read this statistics/data page and create an authority-building social post.

## My Details
- Niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- Brand: [YOUR BRAND]
- Audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- What I sell: [YOUR OFFER]

## Step 1: Extract
- Pull out the 3-5 most relevant statistics for my audience
- Prioritise stats that are surprising, counterintuitive, or validate a pain point
- Note the source and year for credibility

## Step 2: Build the Post

### Hook
- Lead with the single most shocking or compelling stat
- Frame it as a problem, wake-up call, or opportunity
- Make it impossible to scroll past

### Body
- Connect the stats to a narrative your audience cares about
- Add my perspective - what these numbers actually mean in practice
- Bridge from the data to a practical insight or action step
- Keep it under 150 words

### Close
- End with a question that invites discussion OR
- A clear takeaway that positions me as someone who understands the data
- Soft CTA to [YOUR OFFER] if relevant

## Step 3: Also Provide
- 3 alternative hooks using different stats from the page
- A suggested visual format (text graphic, chart, infographic direction)
- The stat citations formatted for the post
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A complete authority post built around hard data
- The most relevant statistics extracted and prioritised for your audience
- A hook that leads with the most compelling number
- Your perspective woven into the data so it's not just a stat dump
- Three alternative hooks to test different angles
- Citation formatting and visual direction suggestions

## Pro Tips
- Stats posts consistently outperform opinion posts for reach - the algorithm rewards saves and shares
- Pair the post with a simple text graphic showing the key stat for higher engagement
- Run this on your industry's annual report every year for a guaranteed authority piece
