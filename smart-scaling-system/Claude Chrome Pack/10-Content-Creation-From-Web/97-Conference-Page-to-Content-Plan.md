# Prompt 97: Conference Page to Content Plan

**Category:** Content Creation from the Web
**Where to Use:** Event/conference schedule page
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Turns a conference or event schedule page into a full content plan covering pre-event, live, and post-event content. Gives you a structured plan for maximising visibility around any industry event - whether you're attending, speaking, or just following along.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Turn This Conference Schedule Into a Content Plan

Read this event/conference page and create a full content plan around it.

## My Details
- Brand: [YOUR BRAND]
- Niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- Audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- My offer: [YOUR OFFER]
- Am I attending/speaking/watching remotely: [ATTENDING / SPEAKING / REMOTE]

## Content Plan Structure

### Pre-Event (1-2 weeks before)
- 3 posts building anticipation and positioning me as someone worth following at this event
- Include: what I'm looking forward to, predictions, questions I want answered
- Tag relevant speakers and organisers where appropriate

### Live Coverage (during the event)
- 5 real-time content angles I can capture on the day
- Include: key takeaway posts, behind-the-scenes angles, speaker quote posts
- Format each as a ready-to-post template I just need to fill in on the spot

### Post-Event (1 week after)
- 3 posts for reflection and authority building
- Include: top 3 takeaways post, contrarian opinion on something discussed, how it connects to my work
- One longer-form piece (LinkedIn article or blog post outline)

### Networking Content
- 2 posts tagging or mentioning speakers/attendees
- Connection-building, not name-dropping

## Rules
- Every post must tie back to my niche and position me as an authority
- No generic "excited to attend" content - every post needs a specific angle
- Include suggested post times for maximum engagement
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A complete content plan spanning pre-event, live, and post-event phases
- 13+ ready-to-use post templates and outlines
- Real-time content angles with fill-in-the-blank templates for live posting
- Networking content that builds relationships without being cringe
- Suggested posting times for each phase
- Every piece tied to your niche and authority positioning

## Pro Tips
- Even if you're not attending, use the "remote" option to create commentary content that positions you in the conversation
- Screenshot speaker slides during live sessions for instant post visuals
- The post-event "contrarian take" post almost always outperforms the recap posts - lean into that
