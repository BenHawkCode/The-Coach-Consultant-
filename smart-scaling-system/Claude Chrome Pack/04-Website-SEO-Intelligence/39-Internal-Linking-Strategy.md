# Prompt 39: Internal Linking Strategy

**Category:** Website & SEO Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Your blog index page
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Medium

## What This Does
Analyses your blog index to map relationships between posts, identify linking opportunities you are missing, and build a strategic internal linking plan. Strong internal linking helps search engines understand your site structure, distributes page authority, and keeps visitors on your site longer.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Internal Linking Strategy Builder

Analyse every blog post title and description visible on this blog index page. Build me a complete internal linking strategy based on topic relationships.

## What To Analyse
- Topic relationships: Which posts cover related subjects and should link to each other?
- Content clusters: Group posts into topic clusters with a clear pillar post for each
- Link gaps: Which posts likely have zero or minimal internal links based on their isolation from other topics?
- Authority flow: Which posts should receive the most internal links (highest-value content)?
- Anchor text: What natural anchor text should each link use?

## My Details
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My website: [YOUR WEBSITE]
- My most important page (the one I want ranking highest): [YOUR KEY PAGE / OFFER PAGE]
- My primary offer: [YOUR OFFER]

## Output Format
1. List every post title visible on this page
2. Group them into topic clusters (3-7 clusters depending on content volume) with a pillar post identified for each
3. Create a linking matrix: for each post, list which other posts it should link to and the suggested anchor text
4. Identify "orphan" posts that do not fit any cluster - suggest which cluster to connect them to
5. Rank the top 10 highest-priority links to add first (based on SEO impact)
6. For each priority link, specify: source post, target post, suggested anchor text, and where in the source post to place it
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A full inventory of your blog content organised by topic cluster
- Pillar posts identified for each cluster to focus your authority
- A linking matrix showing exactly which posts should connect to which
- Orphan content flagged with recommendations to integrate it
- The top 10 highest-impact links to add immediately, with exact placement instructions
- Suggested anchor text for every link so you can implement without guessing

## Pro Tips
- Start with the top 10 priority links - you will see ranking improvements within weeks
- Link your blog posts to your services or offer pages too, not just to other blog posts
- Revisit this every time you publish 5-10 new posts to keep your linking structure tight
