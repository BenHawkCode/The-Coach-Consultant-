# Prompt 38: FAQ Page Generator

**Category:** Website & SEO Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Google search "[your niche] questions"
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Extracts the real questions people are searching for in your niche from Google's own results - "People Also Ask" boxes, autocomplete suggestions, and related searches. Then turns them into a structured FAQ page with SEO-optimised answers designed to capture featured snippets and drive organic traffic.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# FAQ Page Generator

Analyse this Google search results page and extract every question, query suggestion, and related search visible. Then turn them into a complete FAQ page I can add to my website.

## What To Extract
- "People Also Ask" questions (expand and capture all visible ones)
- Related searches at the bottom of the page
- Any questions visible in search result titles or snippets
- Autocomplete-style suggestions if visible

## FAQ Writing Rules
- Group questions into 3-5 logical themes
- Answer each question in 2-4 sentences - concise and direct
- First sentence must directly answer the question (for featured snippet eligibility)
- Use natural language, not keyword-stuffed robotic copy
- Include my brand perspective where relevant - position me as the authority
- End 2-3 answers with a soft mention of my offer as the natural next step

## My Details
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My brand: [YOUR BRAND]
- My target audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- My primary offer: [YOUR OFFER]
- My website: [YOUR WEBSITE]

## Output Format
1. List every question extracted from this page (raw list)
2. Group them into 3-5 themed sections with a section title for each
3. Write the full FAQ content - question and answer for each, formatted in proper FAQ structure
4. Mark which 5 questions have the highest featured snippet potential
5. Include schema markup recommendations (FAQ schema) for SEO
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A complete list of real questions your audience is actively searching
- Questions grouped into logical themes for easy navigation
- Full FAQ content written and ready to publish on your site
- Featured snippet-optimised answers for the highest-opportunity questions
- Schema markup guidance so search engines display your FAQs in rich results
- Soft CTA placements woven naturally into relevant answers

## Pro Tips
- Search 3-5 different variations of your niche query and merge the results for a comprehensive FAQ page
- Add FAQ schema markup to get your answers displayed directly in Google search results
- Update this quarterly - new questions emerge as your market evolves
