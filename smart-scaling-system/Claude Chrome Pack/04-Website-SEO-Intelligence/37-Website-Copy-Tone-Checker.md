# Prompt 37: Website Copy Tone Checker

**Category:** Website & SEO Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Any page on your website
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Checks whether your website copy actually sounds like your brand. Identifies sections where the tone drifts, language feels inconsistent, or the writing does not match your audience. Gives you specific rewrites so every page sounds like it was written by the same person with the same intent.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Website Copy Tone Checker

Analyse every piece of copy on this page and assess whether it sounds consistent, on-brand, and appropriate for my target audience. Flag anything that feels off.

## What To Check
- Tone consistency: Does the entire page sound like it was written by one person with one voice?
- Brand alignment: Does the language match the brand personality I describe below?
- Audience appropriateness: Would my ideal client feel spoken to directly, or does it feel generic?
- Formality level: Is it too stiff, too casual, or inconsistent across sections?
- Jargon and complexity: Is there industry language my audience would not understand?
- Emotional resonance: Does the copy connect with the reader's pain points and desires?
- Passive vs active voice: Flag any passive constructions that weaken the messaging

## My Brand Voice
- My brand: [YOUR BRAND]
- My tone: [DESCRIBE YOUR TONE - e.g. "Direct, conversational, confident but not arrogant, Yorkshire-blunt"]
- My audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- Words I always use: [LIST 3-5 WORDS OR PHRASES CORE TO YOUR BRAND]
- Words I never use: [LIST 3-5 WORDS OR PHRASES YOU AVOID]

## Output Format
1. Rate overall tone consistency (1-10) with a one-paragraph summary
2. Highlight every section or sentence that sounds off-brand - quote the exact text and explain why
3. For each flagged section, provide a rewritten version that matches my stated brand voice
4. Identify any patterns (e.g. "Your headers are confident but your body copy becomes apologetic")
5. Give me 3 tone rules specific to my brand that I should apply to all future website copy
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- An overall tone consistency score with detailed reasoning
- Every off-brand sentence flagged with the exact text quoted
- Rewritten alternatives for each flagged section matching your voice
- Pattern analysis revealing where your tone breaks down most often
- Three custom tone rules tailored to your brand for future writing
- A clear picture of how your audience actually experiences your copy

## Pro Tips
- Run this on every page of your site and collect the flagged patterns - they reveal your biggest consistency gap
- Save the 3 tone rules it generates and paste them into every future copywriting brief
- Run this after a website redesign or when multiple people have contributed copy
