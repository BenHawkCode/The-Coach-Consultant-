# Prompt 34: SEO Content Gap Finder

**Category:** Website & SEO Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Competitor blog or Google search results
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Medium

## What This Does
Analyses competitor content or search results to find the topics, keywords, and questions your website is not covering. Identifies content gaps you can fill to capture traffic your competitors are getting - and organises them into actionable topic clusters you can build out systematically.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# SEO Content Gap Finder

Analyse the content on this page to identify content opportunities I am missing. I want to find gaps in my content strategy that my competitors are already filling.

## What To Analyse
- Topic clusters: What broad themes does this content cover? Which ones am I likely missing?
- Specific keywords: What search terms is this content targeting? List them explicitly
- Underserved questions: What questions does this content answer that I probably have not addressed?
- Content depth: Where does this content go deep that most competitors stay surface-level?
- Content format: What formats are being used (guides, lists, comparisons, tutorials)?

## My Details
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My target audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- My website: [YOUR WEBSITE]
- Topics I already cover: [LIST 3-5 TOPICS YOU ALREADY HAVE CONTENT ON]

## Output Format
1. List every topic/keyword this page targets (be specific - actual phrases, not categories)
2. Group them into 3-5 topic clusters with a theme name for each
3. For each cluster, identify which topics I am likely NOT covering based on what I told you above
4. Rank the gaps by estimated search demand (High / Medium / Low) and competition difficulty
5. Give me a prioritised content plan: 10 specific article titles I should write, ordered by impact
6. For each article title, include the primary keyword to target and a one-line content angle
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A complete keyword and topic map of what your competitor covers
- Topic clusters grouped by theme with clear gap identification
- Each gap ranked by search demand and competition difficulty
- A prioritised 10-article content plan ready to execute
- Specific primary keywords and content angles for each piece
- A clear picture of where you are losing traffic to competitors

## Pro Tips
- Run this on 3-4 different competitors and merge the results - patterns across multiple competitors reveal the strongest opportunities
- Focus on the "High demand / Low competition" gaps first for quick wins
- Use the topic clusters to plan content series rather than one-off posts
