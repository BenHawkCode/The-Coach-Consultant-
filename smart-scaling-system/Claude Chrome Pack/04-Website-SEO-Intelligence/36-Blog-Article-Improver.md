# Prompt 36: Blog Article Improver

**Category:** Website & SEO Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Your published blog post
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Takes an existing blog post and identifies exactly what to improve for better rankings, readability, and conversions. Covers structure, SEO, content depth, internal linking, and CTA effectiveness - giving you a specific improvement plan rather than a vague list of suggestions.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Blog Article Improver

Analyse this published blog post and give me a specific improvement plan to increase its rankings, readability, and conversion rate. Be direct about what is weak.

## Analyse These Areas
- Readability: Sentence length, paragraph length, jargon, scan-ability. Would someone actually read this or bounce?
- Structure: Is the heading hierarchy logical (H1 > H2 > H3)? Are sections in the right order? Does the intro hook the reader?
- SEO optimisation: Keyword usage in title, headings, first paragraph, and throughout. Is it natural or forced?
- Content depth: Does this thoroughly answer the reader's question, or does it stay surface-level? What is missing?
- Internal linking opportunities: What other pages or posts on my site should this link to?
- CTA effectiveness: Does the post guide readers toward a next step? Is the CTA relevant to the content?
- Formatting: Use of bullet points, bold text, images, and white space

## My Details
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My target audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- The primary keyword I want this post to rank for: [TARGET KEYWORD]
- My website: [YOUR WEBSITE]

## Output Format
1. Rate each area (Strong / Needs Work / Weak) with one specific reason
2. Rewrite the introduction to hook readers faster (max 3 short paragraphs)
3. Suggest a restructured heading outline if the current one is weak
4. Identify 3-5 content gaps - specific subtopics or questions this post should cover but does not
5. Write a stronger CTA section for the end of the post
6. List 5 internal linking suggestions (describe the target page and suggest anchor text)
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A rated audit of every element affecting the post's performance
- A rewritten introduction that hooks readers immediately
- A restructured heading outline if the current one underperforms
- Specific content gaps with subtopics to add for better depth and rankings
- A stronger end-of-post CTA section ready to drop in
- Internal linking suggestions with anchor text recommendations

## Pro Tips
- Start with your highest-traffic posts - small improvements on popular content have the biggest compounding effect
- Add the content gap sections as new H2s rather than rewriting the entire post
- Run this on posts that rank on page 2 of Google - they are closest to breaking through to page 1
