# Prompt 32: Homepage Messaging Audit

**Category:** Website & SEO Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Your homepage
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Audits your homepage messaging for clarity, persuasion, and conversion effectiveness. Analyses your headline, value proposition, above-the-fold impact, benefit communication, and CTA strength - then gives you rewritten alternatives that hit harder with your specific audience.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Homepage Messaging Audit

You are a direct-response copywriter. Analyse every piece of messaging on this homepage and tell me exactly where it is weak, vague, or failing to convert.

## Analyse These Elements
- Headline: Is it clear what I do, who I help, and why they should care - within 5 seconds?
- Subheadline: Does it support the headline or waste space?
- Above-the-fold impact: Would a stranger immediately understand the offer and feel compelled to scroll?
- Value proposition: Is the core benefit crystal clear or buried in jargon?
- Benefit communication: Are benefits specific and outcome-driven, or generic and forgettable?
- CTA strength: Are the calls to action compelling, specific, and low-friction?
- Social proof placement: Is trust built before asking for action?

## My Details
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My brand name: [YOUR BRAND]
- My target audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- My primary offer: [YOUR OFFER]

## Output Format
1. Rate each element above (Strong / Needs Work / Weak) with a one-line reason
2. Identify the single biggest messaging gap costing me conversions
3. Rewrite my headline - give me 3 alternatives that are specific, benefit-led, and audience-aware
4. Rewrite my primary CTA - give me 3 alternatives that reduce friction and increase urgency
5. Rewrite any subheadline or supporting copy that scored "Weak"
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Element-by-element rating of your current homepage messaging
- The single biggest conversion gap you need to close immediately
- Three rewritten headline options tailored to your audience
- Three stronger CTA alternatives with lower friction
- Rewritten supporting copy for any weak sections
- A clear before/after comparison so you can see the difference

## Pro Tips
- Screenshot your current homepage before making changes so you can A/B test the results
- Test the rewritten headlines as ad copy too - strong headlines work across channels
- Run this quarterly as your offer and audience evolve
