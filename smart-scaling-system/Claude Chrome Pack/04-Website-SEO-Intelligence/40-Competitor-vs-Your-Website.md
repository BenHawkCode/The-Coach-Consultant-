# Prompt 40: Competitor vs Your Website Comparison

**Category:** Website & SEO Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Competitor page (then run on yours)
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Medium

## What This Does
Runs a structured side-by-side comparison between a competitor's page and yours. Analyses messaging strength, UX quality, trust signals, CTA effectiveness, and content depth - then gives you specific actions to close every gap where you are falling behind and double down where you are ahead.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Competitor vs My Website - Side-by-Side Analysis

Analyse this competitor's page in detail. I will then run this same prompt on my own page so I can compare the two. Score every element so I can see exactly where I win and where I lose.

## Analyse & Score (1-10 each)
- Messaging strength: Is the value proposition immediately clear and compelling?
- Headline impact: Does the headline stop you, make you curious, or fall flat?
- UX quality: Navigation, layout, visual hierarchy, readability, mobile-friendliness
- Trust signals: Testimonials, case studies, logos, certifications, social proof placement
- CTA effectiveness: Clarity, placement, frequency, urgency, friction level
- Content depth: Does the page thoroughly address what the visitor came for?
- Objection handling: Does the page pre-empt reasons someone would leave without acting?
- Design professionalism: Does it look credible, modern, and trustworthy?
- Differentiation: Is it clear why this business is different from alternatives?
- Speed to value: How quickly does a visitor understand what they get and why it matters?

## My Details
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My brand: [YOUR BRAND]
- My target audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- My website: [YOUR WEBSITE]
- My primary offer: [YOUR OFFER]

## Output Format
1. Score each element (1-10) with a one-line justification
2. Identify this competitor's 3 biggest strengths (what they do better than most)
3. Identify this competitor's 3 biggest weaknesses (where they are vulnerable)
4. For each weakness, suggest how I could exploit that gap on my own site
5. Overall competitive threat level: Low / Medium / High - with reasoning
6. List 5 specific actions I should take on MY site based on what this competitor does well

## IMPORTANT
Save this full analysis. When I run this prompt on my own page next, I will ask you to compare the two scorecards and give me a gap-closing action plan.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A 10-category scored analysis of your competitor's page
- Their three biggest strengths you need to match or exceed
- Their three biggest weaknesses you can exploit immediately
- Specific actions to take on your own site based on what works for them
- A competitive threat assessment so you know how seriously to treat them
- A framework to run the same analysis on your own page for direct comparison

## Pro Tips
- Run this on your competitor's page first, then immediately run it on your own equivalent page and ask for a comparison
- Do this for your top 3 competitors to spot patterns - if all three do something you do not, that is a priority gap
- Focus your effort on categories where the score gap is 3+ points - those are the high-impact opportunities
