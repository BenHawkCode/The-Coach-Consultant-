# Prompt 33: Services Page Optimiser

**Category:** Website & SEO Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Your services page
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Tears apart your services page and rebuilds the messaging for maximum clarity and conversion. Analyses how you present your offers, frame benefits, handle objections, and guide visitors toward action - then gives you specific copy and structural changes to implement immediately.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Services Page Optimiser

Analyse this services page as a conversion rate optimisation specialist. My goal is to turn visitors into enquiries or buyers. Be direct about what is not working.

## Analyse These Areas
- Offer clarity: Can a visitor understand exactly what they get within 10 seconds?
- Benefit framing: Are outcomes specific and tangible, or vague and generic?
- Pricing presentation: If pricing is shown, is it anchored and justified? If hidden, should it be visible?
- Objection handling: Does the page address the top 3-5 reasons someone would NOT buy?
- CTA placement: Are there enough CTAs? Are they in the right positions? Do they match the buyer's stage?
- Trust and proof: Are testimonials, case studies, or results placed where hesitation happens?
- Service differentiation: Is it clear why someone should choose me over alternatives?

## My Details
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My brand: [YOUR BRAND]
- My target audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- My main service/offer: [YOUR OFFER]
- My website: [YOUR WEBSITE]

## Output Format
1. Rate each area (Strong / Needs Work / Weak) with specific reasoning
2. Identify the top 3 conversion killers on this page
3. Suggest an optimised page structure (section order) based on proven service page frameworks
4. Rewrite the weakest sections with copy I can use directly
5. List 3 objections my audience likely has that this page does not address - and write the copy to handle them
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A rated breakdown of every services page element
- The top 3 specific things killing your conversions
- An optimised page structure you can implement immediately
- Ready-to-use rewritten copy for your weakest sections
- Objection-handling copy blocks you can drop straight into the page
- Clear differentiation suggestions to separate you from competitors

## Pro Tips
- Run this on a competitor's services page first, then yours - the contrast reveals blind spots fast
- The objection-handling copy it generates makes excellent FAQ content too
- If you have multiple service tiers, run this prompt separately on each one
