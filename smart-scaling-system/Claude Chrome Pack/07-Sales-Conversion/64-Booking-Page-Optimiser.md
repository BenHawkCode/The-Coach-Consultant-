# Prompt 64: Booking Page Optimiser

**Category:** Sales & Conversion
**Where to Use:** Your booking/calendar page
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Audits your booking or calendar page for friction points, weak copy, missing trust signals, and poor user experience. Gives you specific fixes to increase the percentage of visitors who actually complete a booking.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Booking Page Conversion Audit

Analyse this booking/calendar page and identify everything that could be reducing conversions. My brand is [YOUR BRAND] and this page targets [YOUR AUDIENCE] in [YOUR NICHE].

## First Impression (Above the Fold)
- Is the headline clear about what they are booking and why it matters?
- Does the page immediately communicate value or just ask for time?
- Rate the visual hierarchy - can someone understand the page in 5 seconds?

## Friction Points
- List every point where a visitor might hesitate, get confused, or leave
- Flag unnecessary form fields that could be removed or simplified
- Identify any steps in the booking flow that add friction without adding value
- Note any technical issues (slow loading, broken elements, mobile problems)

## Copy & Messaging
- Is the copy benefit-focused or feature-focused?
- Does it address the prospect's main objection to booking?
- Is there a clear statement of what happens during and after the call?
- Rate the call-to-action button text - is it specific or generic?

## Trust Signals
- What social proof is present (testimonials, logos, results)?
- What is missing that would increase confidence in booking?
- Is there a photo or video of who they will be speaking with?
- Are there any guarantees or risk-reversal elements?

## Pre-Qualification
- Does the page filter out unqualified leads before they book?
- Suggest 3-5 pre-qualification questions that would improve lead quality
- Are expectations set for what the call will cover?

## Specific Fixes
- List 10 specific changes ranked by likely impact on conversion rate
- For each fix, explain what to change and why it matters
- Flag any quick wins that could be implemented in under 30 minutes
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A complete friction audit showing exactly where people drop off
- Copy improvements that shift from feature-focused to benefit-focused
- Missing trust signals you can add immediately
- Pre-qualification questions to improve lead quality
- 10 prioritised fixes ranked by conversion impact
- Quick wins you can implement the same day

## Pro Tips
- Test your booking page on mobile before running this audit - most prospects will see it on their phone first
- Add one testimonial from a client who specifically mentions the value of the call itself, not just the end result
- Run this audit again after implementing fixes to catch anything you missed in the first pass
