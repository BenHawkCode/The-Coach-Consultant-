# Prompt 65: Competitor Funnel Mapper

**Category:** Sales & Conversion
**Where to Use:** Competitor opt-in or sales page
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Medium

## What This Does
Reverse-engineers a competitor's entire sales funnel from a single page. Maps their entry point, nurture strategy, upsell indicators, and conversion path so you can identify what is working, what is missing, and where you can outperform them.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Competitor Funnel Reverse-Engineering

Analyse this page and map out the complete sales funnel this competitor is running. I operate in [YOUR NICHE] serving [YOUR AUDIENCE].

## Entry Point Analysis
- What is the primary offer on this page (lead magnet, free trial, consultation, direct sale)?
- What is the hook that gets people into the funnel?
- How are they positioning the entry offer - what promise are they making?
- What is the perceived value vs what they are actually giving away?

## Nurture Sequence Signals
- What email capture mechanisms are visible on this page?
- Based on the page structure, what type of follow-up sequence are they likely running?
- Are there signs of segmentation (different paths for different audiences)?
- What content types are they using to build trust (blog, video, case studies)?

## Upsell & Conversion Path
- What paid offers are visible or implied from this page?
- Map the likely journey from this page to their core paid offer
- Identify any order bumps, upsells, or cross-sells indicated on the page
- What pricing strategy are they using (tiered, single offer, high-ticket)?

## Funnel Strengths
- What is this funnel doing well that you should take note of?
- Which elements would work well for [YOUR BRAND]?
- What psychological triggers are they using effectively?

## Funnel Gaps & Weaknesses
- Where are the obvious drop-off points in their funnel?
- What objections are they failing to address?
- What trust-building elements are missing?
- Where could a competitor (you) outperform them?

## Your Funnel Opportunities
- Based on this analysis, suggest 5 specific improvements for your own funnel
- Identify the single biggest advantage you could build over this competitor
- Recommend which elements to adapt and which to avoid entirely

Present this as a visual funnel map with stages clearly labelled.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A complete map of their funnel from entry point to paid offer
- Nurture strategy signals including email capture and follow-up approach
- Their upsell and pricing strategy decoded
- Specific strengths worth learning from and gaps worth exploiting
- Five actionable improvements for your own funnel
- A clear competitive advantage you can build

## Pro Tips
- Run this on 3-4 competitors in the same niche and compare their funnel structures side by side to spot industry patterns
- Actually opt in to their lead magnet so you can map the email nurture sequence over the following 2 weeks
- Focus on what they are NOT doing rather than copying what they are - the gaps are where your opportunity lives
