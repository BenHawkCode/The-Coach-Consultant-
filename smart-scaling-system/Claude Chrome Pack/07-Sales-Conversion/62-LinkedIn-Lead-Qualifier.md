# Prompt 62: LinkedIn Lead Qualifier

**Category:** Sales & Conversion
**Where to Use:** Prospect's LinkedIn profile
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Qualifies a LinkedIn lead in seconds by analysing their profile for decision-making authority, company size signals, pain point indicators, and mutual connections. Gives you a personalised outreach angle so every message you send feels relevant and researched.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# LinkedIn Lead Qualification

Analyse this LinkedIn profile and qualify this person as a potential lead for [YOUR OFFER] in [YOUR NICHE].

## Role & Authority Assessment
- What is their current role and how long have they been in it?
- Are they a decision maker, influencer, or end user?
- Do they have budget authority based on their title and seniority?
- What does their career trajectory tell us about their ambitions?

## Company Signals
- What company are they at and what does it do?
- Estimate the company size based on available signals (employee count, industry)
- Is this company in a growth phase, stable, or potentially struggling?

## Pain Point Indicators
- Based on their role in [YOUR NICHE], what are their 3 most likely daily frustrations?
- Do any of their posts, articles, or activity reveal specific challenges?
- What skills or endorsements suggest areas they are actively developing?

## Connection Opportunities
- Note any mutual connections, shared groups, or common interests
- Flag any recent posts, comments, or shares I could reference in outreach
- Identify shared experiences (education, past companies, certifications)

## Personalised Outreach Angle
- Write a 3-line connection request message that references something specific from their profile
- Suggest a follow-up message for after they accept
- Recommend the single strongest hook for why [YOUR OFFER] is relevant to them right now

## Lead Score
- Rate this lead 1-10 based on fit for [YOUR OFFER]
- List the top 3 reasons they are or are not a strong fit
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A clear assessment of their decision-making power and budget authority
- Company context including size, growth stage, and industry fit
- Specific pain points mapped to their role and your offer
- Mutual connections and shared interests for warm outreach
- A ready-to-send personalised connection request
- A lead score so you can prioritise your outreach time

## Pro Tips
- Batch-process 10-15 profiles in a row and rank them by lead score before sending any messages
- Copy the outreach angle into your CRM notes so your follow-up stays consistent with the initial message
- Check their recent activity tab first - a prospect who posts regularly is warmer than one who has been silent for months
