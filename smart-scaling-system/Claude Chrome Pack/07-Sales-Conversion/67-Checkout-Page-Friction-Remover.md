# Prompt 67: Checkout Page Friction Remover

**Category:** Sales & Conversion
**Where to Use:** Your checkout/payment page
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Audits your checkout page for conversion killers that cause people to abandon at the final step. Identifies missing trust signals, confusing pricing, unnecessary form fields, and security concerns that cost you sales you have already earned.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Checkout Page Conversion Audit

Analyse this checkout/payment page and identify every element that could be causing abandoned purchases. My brand is [YOUR BRAND] selling [YOUR OFFER] to [YOUR AUDIENCE].

## Trust & Security Signals
- What trust badges, security icons, or payment logos are visible?
- Is there an SSL indicator and does the page feel secure?
- What guarantees or refund policies are displayed?
- List every trust signal that is missing and should be added

## Pricing Clarity
- Is the total price immediately clear with no surprises?
- Are there any hidden fees, taxes, or charges that appear late?
- Is the value being reinforced on this page or just the cost?
- Does the pricing include a brief reminder of what they are getting?

## Form Fields & Friction
- List every form field on the page
- Flag which fields are unnecessary and can be removed
- Is the form auto-filling properly for returning visitors?
- How many clicks does it take to complete the purchase?

## Abandonment Risk Factors
- What could cause someone to leave at this exact step?
- Are there any distracting links or navigation that lead away from checkout?
- Is there a clear path back if someone leaves (abandonment recovery)?
- Does the page work smoothly on mobile?

## Order Summary
- Is there a clear summary of what they are purchasing?
- Are the deliverables or inclusions listed?
- Is there a testimonial or result near the buy button?
- Does the page remind them why they wanted this in the first place?

## Specific Fixes
- List 10 changes ranked by likely impact on checkout completion
- Flag any quick wins that take under 15 minutes to implement
- Suggest the single most impactful change to make first
- Recommend a post-purchase confirmation improvement
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A complete trust signal audit with specific additions to make
- Pricing clarity fixes that eliminate surprise abandonment
- Unnecessary form fields flagged for removal
- Every abandonment risk factor identified with solutions
- 10 prioritised fixes ranked by revenue impact
- Quick wins you can implement immediately

## Pro Tips
- The number one checkout killer is unexpected costs appearing at the end - if your price changes between sales page and checkout, you will lose people
- Add a single testimonial next to the payment button that specifically mentions the result, not just satisfaction
- Remove all navigation links from the checkout page - the only options should be complete the purchase or close the tab
