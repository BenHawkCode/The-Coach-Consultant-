# Prompt 68: Thank You Page Maximiser

**Category:** Sales & Conversion
**Where to Use:** Your thank you/confirmation page
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Turns your thank you page from a dead end into a revenue and engagement driver. Identifies opportunities for upsells, community building, referral prompts, and next-step clarity that most business owners completely overlook.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Thank You Page Maximisation Audit

Analyse this thank you/confirmation page and identify every missed opportunity to drive further action. My brand is [YOUR BRAND] and this page appears after [YOUR OFFER] for [YOUR AUDIENCE].

## Next Steps Clarity
- Is it immediately clear what happens next (delivery timeline, access instructions, call details)?
- Are expectations set for when and how they will receive what they signed up for?
- Is there a checklist or simple steps section telling them exactly what to do now?
- Rate the overall clarity - would someone know exactly what to expect after reading this?

## Upsell & Cross-Sell Opportunities
- What related offer could be presented here while buying intent is highest?
- Suggest 3 upsell or cross-sell options that would feel helpful rather than pushy
- What is the ideal price point for an impulse add-on at this stage?
- How should the upsell be framed to complement what they just purchased?

## Community & Engagement
- Is there a link to join a community, group, or social channel?
- Are they being invited to follow on social media or subscribe to content?
- Is there a way for them to connect with other customers or members?
- Suggest 3 engagement hooks that keep them active after the purchase

## Referral & Social Sharing
- Is there a referral prompt or share mechanism on this page?
- What incentive could be offered for referring others?
- Are there pre-written social share messages they can post with one click?
- How could word-of-mouth be activated while their excitement is highest?

## Content & Value Delivery
- Is there any bonus content or quick win delivered on this page?
- Could a short video welcome message increase satisfaction and reduce refunds?
- What immediate value could be provided to reinforce their decision?

## Specific Improvements
- List 8 changes ranked by likely impact on lifetime customer value
- Suggest the single most valuable addition to this page
- Recommend what to remove if anything is creating confusion
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A clarity audit ensuring buyers know exactly what happens next
- Three upsell options positioned as helpful rather than salesy
- Community and engagement hooks to keep new buyers active
- Referral mechanisms to activate word-of-mouth while excitement is high
- Bonus content ideas that reinforce the purchase decision
- Eight prioritised improvements to increase lifetime value

## Pro Tips
- The thank you page is the single most underused page in most funnels - your buyer is at peak excitement and trust, so use it
- A 60-second welcome video from you on this page dramatically reduces refund rates and increases community engagement
- Keep the upsell to one offer maximum - presenting too many options after a purchase creates regret rather than excitement
