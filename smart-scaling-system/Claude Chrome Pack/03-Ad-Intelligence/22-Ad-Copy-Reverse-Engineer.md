# Prompt 22: Ad Copy Reverse Engineer

**Category:** Ad Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Any ad landing page (the page you land on after clicking an ad)
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Breaks down the entire persuasion architecture of an ad landing page - from headline formula to CTA placement. You get the exact copywriting frameworks being used, the objections being handled, and a blueprint you can adapt for your own pages.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Ad Landing Page Copy Reverse-Engineer

Analyse this landing page as if you were a direct response copywriter auditing it for a client. Break down every persuasion element:

## Headline Analysis
- What is the main headline?
- What formula does it follow? (problem-solution, curiosity gap, benefit-driven, social proof, etc.)
- What specific emotion does it target?
- Rate its clarity out of 10 and explain why

## Body Copy Structure
Map out the exact flow of the page from top to bottom:
- What comes after the headline? (subhead, video, bullet points, story?)
- How many distinct sections are there?
- What is the purpose of each section? (build desire, handle objection, create urgency, establish authority)
- Where does the copy shift from education to selling?

## Emotional Triggers
List every emotional trigger used on this page:
- Pain points referenced
- Aspirational outcomes promised
- Fear/urgency elements
- Social proof placements (testimonials, logos, numbers)

## Objection Handling
- What objections does this page anticipate and address?
- Where on the page are they handled?
- What technique is used? (FAQ, guarantee, comparison, testimonial)

## CTA Strategy
- How many CTAs are on the page?
- What does each CTA say?
- Where are they placed relative to the persuasion flow?
- Is there a primary vs secondary CTA?

## Swipe-Worthy Elements
List the 3 strongest copy elements on this page I could adapt for my own landing page in [YOUR NICHE] selling [YOUR OFFER].

For each one, rewrite it adapted to my business.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Headline formula identification with effectiveness rating
- Full page structure map showing the persuasion flow section by section
- Complete emotional trigger inventory with placement locations
- Objection handling strategy breakdown
- CTA placement and copy analysis
- Three adapted copy elements ready to use in your own pages

## Pro Tips
- Run this on the landing pages of ads that have been running for 3+ months - those pages have been tested and optimised
- Compare the output from 3-4 competitor landing pages side by side to spot the patterns that keep repeating (those are proven to work)
- Pay special attention to the objection handling section - those are the real buying barriers in your market
