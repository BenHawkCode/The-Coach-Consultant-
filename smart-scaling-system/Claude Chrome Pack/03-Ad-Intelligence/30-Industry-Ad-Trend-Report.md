# Prompt 30: Industry Ad Trend Report

**Category:** Ad Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Meta Ad Library niche/keyword search results
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Medium

## What This Does
Analyses ads across an entire niche to map industry-wide trends - common offers, pricing patterns, creative formats, messaging themes, and the gaps nobody is filling. This is market research that would cost thousands from an agency, built from real ad data.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Industry Ad Trend Report

Analyse every ad visible on this Meta Ad Library search to build a comprehensive trend report for this niche. I want market-level intelligence, not individual ad analysis.

## Offer Landscape
- What types of offers are being promoted? (free resources, webinars, challenges, low-ticket, high-ticket, SaaS trials, physical products)
- What percentage of ads fall into each offer type?
- Are there pricing signals visible? What price points keep appearing?
- What's the dominant funnel model? (lead magnet > nurture > sale, direct to sale, free trial > paid, etc.)

## Creative Format Trends
- What's the dominant ad format in this niche? (static, video, carousel)
- What visual styles keep appearing? (UGC, professional, text-overlay, lifestyle, screenshot/results)
- Are there production quality patterns? (high-budget vs scrappy)
- What thumbnail/image patterns get used most?

## Messaging Themes
Identify the top 5 messaging themes across all ads:
- What pain points does the entire industry lead with?
- What aspirational outcomes are most commonly promised?
- What language patterns keep repeating across different advertisers?
- Is the niche fear-driven, aspiration-driven, or logic-driven in its messaging?

## Audience Signals
- What age/demographic signals are visible in the creative choices?
- Is the niche speaking to beginners, intermediates, or advanced audiences?
- Are there sub-niches or audience segments getting targeted differently?

## Saturation vs Opportunity
- Which positioning angles are oversaturated? (everyone is saying the same thing)
- Which offer types are overcrowded?
- What messaging themes have become cliches in this niche?
- What gaps exist that NO advertiser is filling?
- What adjacent audiences could be targeted that no one is reaching?

## Trend Direction
Based on the mix of older and newer ads visible:
- What messaging is trending UP? (newer ads leaning this direction)
- What messaging is trending DOWN? (older ads used this but newer ones don't)
- Where is the niche heading in the next 6 months?

## Strategic Recommendations
For someone entering or competing in [YOUR NICHE] with [YOUR OFFER]:
1. The positioning angle most likely to cut through the noise
2. The offer type with the least competition but highest demand signals
3. The creative format to lead with
4. The messaging theme to avoid (too saturated)
5. The audience segment to target first

Format the offer landscape and creative format sections as tables. Write the rest as structured analysis.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Complete offer landscape showing what the entire niche is selling and how
- Creative format trend analysis revealing dominant visual approaches
- Top 5 messaging themes with language pattern breakdowns
- Audience signal mapping across the competitive landscape
- Saturation vs opportunity analysis highlighting gaps to exploit
- Trend direction showing where the niche is heading
- 5 strategic recommendations tailored to your business

## Pro Tips
- Run this quarterly on the same niche search to track how the market evolves - the gaps that exist today will be filled in 6 months, and new ones will open
- Search multiple related keywords for the same niche (e.g. "fitness coaching", "personal trainer", "weight loss programme") to get a complete picture rather than a narrow slice
- Cross-reference the gap analysis with what your audience actually asks you about - if there's a gap in the market AND demand from your audience, that's your strongest play
