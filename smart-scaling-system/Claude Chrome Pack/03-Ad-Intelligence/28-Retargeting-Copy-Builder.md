# Prompt 28: Retargeting Copy Builder

**Category:** Ad Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Competitor's main sales page or your own sales page
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Medium

## What This Does
Analyses a sales page to identify the most common reasons someone would leave without buying, then generates retargeting ad copy that specifically addresses those objections, builds urgency, and uses social proof hooks. You get ready-to-use retargeting ads built on real page intelligence.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Retargeting Ad Copy Builder

Analyse this sales page completely, then generate retargeting ad copy for people who visited but didn't buy.

## Step 1: Page Analysis
Read the entire sales page and identify:
- The main offer (what's being sold, at what price if visible)
- The primary promise/outcome
- All benefits listed
- All objections the page tries to handle
- Any social proof (testimonials, case studies, numbers)
- The main CTA and what it asks visitors to do
- Any guarantee or risk-reversal offered

## Step 2: Drop-Off Diagnosis
Based on the page content, identify the top 5 reasons someone would leave without converting:
1. [Likely objection + why the page didn't fully overcome it]
2. [Likely objection + why the page didn't fully overcome it]
3. [Likely objection + why the page didn't fully overcome it]
4. [Likely objection + why the page didn't fully overcome it]
5. [Likely objection + why the page didn't fully overcome it]

## Step 3: Generate Retargeting Ads
Create 6 retargeting ads (2 per category):

### Category A: Objection Crushers (2 ads)
Address the top objections from Step 2 head-on.

### Category B: Urgency & Scarcity (2 ads)
Create genuine urgency without being manipulative.

### Category C: Social Proof & Results (2 ads)
Lead with proof, outcomes, and credibility.

For each ad, provide:
- **Primary text** (the main ad copy - under 125 characters for the first line, then expand)
- **Headline** (under 40 characters)
- **Description** (under 30 characters)
- **CTA button** (most appropriate option)
- **Creative suggestion** (what image or video style to pair with this)

My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
My offer: [YOUR OFFER]
My brand: [YOUR BRAND]

## Step 4: Retargeting Sequence
Recommend the order these 6 ads should run in a retargeting sequence (Day 1-3, Day 4-7, Day 8-14) and why.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Complete sales page analysis with offer, promise, and proof breakdown
- Top 5 drop-off reasons explaining why visitors leave without buying
- 6 retargeting ads across objection, urgency, and social proof categories
- Full ad copy for each including primary text, headline, description, and CTA
- Creative direction suggestions for each ad
- Recommended retargeting sequence with timing

## Pro Tips
- Run this on YOUR OWN sales page for immediate retargeting copy you can deploy today
- Run it on a competitor's sales page to understand their weak points and create ads that position your offer as the better alternative
- Update the retargeting copy every time you update your sales page - the objections shift as the page changes
