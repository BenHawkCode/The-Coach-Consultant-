# Prompt 29: Ad Spend ROI Analyser

**Category:** Ad Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Your Meta Ads Manager or Google Ads dashboard
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Translates your ad dashboard numbers into plain-English decisions - what's profitable, what's bleeding money, where to increase budget, and where to cut. No more staring at metrics wondering what to do next.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Ad Dashboard ROI Analysis & Action Plan

Read all the campaign data visible on this ad dashboard. I want clear decisions, not just data summaries.

## Data Extraction
For each campaign/ad set/ad visible, capture:
- Campaign name
- Status (active, paused, etc.)
- Spend
- Results/conversions
- Cost per result
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Cost per click (CPC)
- Impressions
- Reach
- Any other metrics visible (ROAS, frequency, relevance score, etc.)

## Performance Ranking
Rank every campaign from best to worst performing based on cost per result. Present as a table with a traffic light system:
- GREEN: Performing well - scale this
- AMBER: Borderline - needs optimisation
- RED: Underperforming - cut or fix immediately

## The Honest Assessment
For each campaign, tell me:
1. Is this actually making money or just generating vanity metrics?
2. What's the real cost to acquire a customer through this campaign?
3. If I could only keep 2 campaigns running, which 2 and why?

## Specific Actions
For GREEN campaigns:
- How much should I increase budget and how quickly?
- What would I duplicate/test next to scale further?

For AMBER campaigns:
- What is the ONE change most likely to move this to green?
- How long should I give it before cutting?

For RED campaigns:
- Should I kill this immediately or is there a quick fix worth trying?
- What went wrong and what should I learn from it?

## Budget Reallocation
Based on this data, if my total ad budget stays the same, show me exactly how I should redistribute spend across campaigns tomorrow.

My monthly ad budget: [YOUR BUDGET]
My target cost per acquisition: [YOUR TARGET CPA]
My average customer value: [YOUR CUSTOMER VALUE]
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Complete data extraction table with all visible metrics
- Traffic-light performance ranking for every campaign
- Honest profitability assessment cutting through vanity metrics
- Specific scaling actions for winning campaigns
- Optimisation or kill decisions for underperformers
- Exact budget reallocation plan you can implement immediately

## Pro Tips
- Run this weekly with the same date range (last 7 days) to track trends over time - one week's data can mislead, but consistent patterns don't lie
- Always fill in your target CPA and customer value - without these, cost per result is meaningless
- Export or screenshot the dashboard before making changes so you have a baseline to compare against next week
