# Prompt 27: Offer Angle Generator

**Category:** Ad Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Meta Ad Library search results page
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Medium

## What This Does
Takes the competitive intelligence from a Meta Ad Library search and generates fresh offer angles, hooks, and creative directions for your own business. Instead of copying competitors, you get differentiated positioning built on top of market intelligence.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Offer Angle Generator From Competitive Intelligence

First, analyse every ad visible on this Meta Ad Library search results page. Then use that intelligence to generate original ad angles for MY business.

## Step 1: Market Intelligence Extraction
Quickly catalogue:
- All distinct offers being promoted (lead magnets, webinars, trials, direct sales)
- All positioning angles used (price, speed, expertise, exclusivity, results)
- All hooks/opening lines
- All emotional triggers (pain points, aspirations, fears)
- All CTAs being used

## Step 2: Gap Identification
Based on what everyone IS doing, identify:
- Positioning angles no one is using
- Pain points no one is addressing
- Audience segments no one is speaking to directly
- Offer types that are missing from this market
- Emotional triggers being overlooked

## Step 3: Generate My Ad Angles
Using the gaps and intelligence above, create 10 original ad angles for my business:

My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
My offer: [YOUR OFFER]
My audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
My website: [YOUR WEBSITE]

For each angle, provide:
1. **Positioning statement** (one sentence - how this angle positions me differently)
2. **Hook** (the first 1-2 lines of ad copy that would stop the scroll)
3. **Core message** (3-4 sentences of body copy)
4. **CTA** (what action I'm asking them to take)
5. **Creative direction** (what the image or video should show)
6. **Why it works** (one sentence on the strategic reasoning)

## Step 4: Priority Ranking
Rank all 10 angles from strongest to weakest. The top 3 should be the ones most likely to stand out in this competitive landscape while resonating with [YOUR AUDIENCE].
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Complete market intelligence summary of current competitive landscape
- Gap analysis revealing untapped positioning opportunities
- 10 fully developed ad angles with hooks, copy, CTAs, and creative direction
- Strategic reasoning behind each angle
- Priority ranking so you know which 3 to test first

## Pro Tips
- Run this on broad niche searches (e.g. "business coaching") AND specific competitor searches separately - broad gives you market gaps, specific gives you head-to-head differentiation
- Take the top 3 ranked angles and test them as your first ad creative batch - you're starting from a position of market intelligence rather than guesswork
- Revisit quarterly as the competitive landscape shifts and new gaps emerge
