# Prompt 24: Landing Page Conversion Audit

**Category:** Ad Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Any ad landing page or sales page
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Runs a full conversion rate optimisation audit on any landing page - covering above the fold, trust signals, copy flow, form friction, and CTA strength. You get specific, prioritised recommendations to improve conversions, not vague advice.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Landing Page Conversion Audit

Audit this landing page as a conversion rate optimisation specialist. Be brutally honest - I want actionable fixes, not compliments.

## Above The Fold (First Screen)
- Is the value proposition clear within 3 seconds?
- Does the headline communicate a specific outcome?
- Is there a clear CTA visible without scrolling?
- Does the visual support or distract from the message?
- Rate the above-the-fold experience out of 10

## Trust & Credibility Signals
- What social proof is present? (testimonials, logos, case studies, numbers, reviews)
- Is it placed effectively or buried?
- Are there any credibility gaps? (claims without proof, missing trust badges, no faces on testimonials)
- What trust signals are MISSING that should be here?

## Copy Flow & Persuasion
- Does the page follow a logical persuasion sequence?
- Map the flow: what emotion does each section target?
- Where does the copy lose momentum or get boring?
- Are benefits concrete and specific, or vague and generic?
- Is there a clear "aha moment" on the page?

## Form & CTA Analysis
- How many form fields are there? (each field adds friction)
- What does the CTA button say? Is it benefit-driven or generic?
- How many CTAs are on the page and where?
- Is there a micro-commitment option (quiz, free resource) or just the big ask?

## Friction & Drop-Off Risks
- What would make someone leave this page without converting?
- Are there any confusing elements, dead-end links, or distractions?
- Is the page doing too much or asking for too much too soon?
- Does the page load fast and look clean, or is it cluttered?

## Mobile Considerations
- Does the visible layout suggest mobile-friendliness?
- Are buttons large enough for thumb tapping?
- Is text readable without zooming?

## Priority Fixes
Give me a ranked list of the TOP 5 changes that would have the biggest impact on conversion rate. For each:
1. What to change
2. Why it matters
3. What to replace it with (specific copy or layout suggestion)

My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
My audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Above-the-fold effectiveness score with specific issues identified
- Trust signal audit highlighting what's present and what's missing
- Copy flow map showing where persuasion momentum breaks down
- Form friction analysis with field-by-field recommendations
- Mobile readiness assessment
- Ranked top 5 priority fixes with exact replacement suggestions

## Pro Tips
- Run this on your OWN landing page first before auditing competitors - the fixes will directly impact your revenue
- Run this on the same page on both desktop and mobile (resize your browser) to catch responsive design issues
- After implementing changes, run the audit again to verify improvements and catch new issues
