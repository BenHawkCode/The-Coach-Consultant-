# Prompt 50: Newsletter Monetisation Auditor

**Category:** Email & Newsletter Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Newsletter archive page
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Analyses how a newsletter makes money by identifying every revenue stream - sponsorships, affiliate links, product mentions, paid tiers, and conversion points. You get a full monetisation model breakdown plus specific revenue ideas you can apply to your own newsletter.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Newsletter Monetisation Auditor

Analyse the newsletter content visible on this page and identify every way this publication generates revenue. I want to understand their full monetisation model so I can apply the best strategies to my own.

## Identify These Revenue Streams
- Sponsorships: Are there sponsored sections, banner ads, or branded content? How are they integrated?
- Affiliate links: Any product recommendations with tracking links or discount codes?
- Product mentions: Are they promoting their own products, courses, services, or events?
- Paid tiers: Any references to premium content, subscriber-only access, or upgrade options?
- Conversion points: Where exactly are readers being directed to spend money?
- Community monetisation: Paid communities, memberships, or exclusive groups?
- Lead generation: Are they driving readers to webinars, calls, or opt-ins for higher-ticket offers?

## My Details
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- My offer: [YOUR OFFER]
- My current newsletter setup: [e.g. free weekly newsletter / no newsletter yet / paid newsletter]
- My subscriber count: [approximate number]

## Output Format
1. Every revenue stream identified with specific examples from the page
2. Estimated monetisation priority ranking (primary income to supplementary)
3. How seamlessly each monetisation method is integrated into the content
4. Revenue opportunities they are missing
5. Top 5 monetisation tactics I should implement for my newsletter, ranked by ease and potential return
6. A suggested monetisation rollout plan starting from where I am now
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A complete map of every revenue stream with specific examples
- Priority ranking of their income sources from primary to supplementary
- Integration quality analysis showing how naturally they monetise
- Missed opportunities they have not capitalised on
- Five monetisation tactics tailored to your niche and subscriber size
- A phased rollout plan so you know what to implement first

## Pro Tips
- Audit newsletters across different niches - the best monetisation ideas often come from outside your industry
- Look at newsletters with similar subscriber counts to yours for the most realistic revenue models
- Start with one monetisation method, optimise it, then layer on the next - do not try everything at once
