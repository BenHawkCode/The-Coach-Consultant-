# Prompt 41: Competitor Newsletter Breakdown

**Category:** Email & Newsletter Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Competitor newsletter viewed in browser
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Tears apart a competitor's newsletter to expose their entire email strategy - structure, tone, content mix, CTAs, and how they monetise their list. You get a clear breakdown of what is working, what is weak, and exactly what you should be borrowing for your own emails.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Competitor Newsletter Breakdown

Analyse this newsletter in full. I want to understand exactly how this competitor structures, writes, and monetises their email list.

## Analyse These Areas
- Structure: How is the email laid out? Sections, headers, content blocks, visual elements
- Tone & voice: What writing style are they using? Formal, casual, authoritative, friendly?
- Content mix: What ratio of value, promotion, storytelling, and news are they using?
- CTAs: How many calls to action? Where are they placed? What language do they use?
- Monetisation: Are they selling products, promoting affiliates, running sponsorships, or driving traffic?
- Subscriber value: What is the reader actually getting from this email? Would they look forward to the next one?
- Subject line & preview text: If visible, assess the open-rate appeal

## My Details
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- My offer: [YOUR OFFER]

## Output Format
1. Full structural breakdown with section-by-section analysis
2. Tone and voice profile (3-5 defining characteristics)
3. Content mix percentage breakdown (value vs promotion vs storytelling vs other)
4. CTA audit - every call to action listed with placement and effectiveness rating
5. Monetisation strategy summary
6. "Steal this" list - 5 specific tactics worth adopting for my own newsletter
7. "Avoid this" list - 3 weaknesses or mistakes to learn from
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A section-by-section structural breakdown of the entire newsletter
- Tone and voice characteristics you can benchmark against your own
- Content mix ratios showing their value-to-promotion balance
- Every CTA mapped with placement strategy and effectiveness notes
- A clear monetisation model breakdown
- Five specific tactics worth stealing and three mistakes to avoid

## Pro Tips
- Save breakdowns from 3-5 competitors to spot patterns across your industry
- Pay closest attention to their CTA placement and language - that is where the money is made
- Run this on the same competitor monthly to track how their strategy evolves
