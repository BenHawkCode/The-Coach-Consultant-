# Prompt 44: Cold Email Template Scraper

**Category:** Email & Newsletter Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Any cold email examples page
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Analyses cold email templates and examples to extract what actually works - the structure patterns, personalisation hooks, CTA styles, and follow-up sequences behind high-performing outreach. You get a proven framework you can adapt for your own cold outreach instead of guessing.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Cold Email Template Scraper

Analyse the cold email templates and examples on this page. I want to extract every usable pattern, structure, and technique so I can build my own high-converting outreach.

## Analyse These Elements
- Opening lines: How are they grabbing attention in the first sentence?
- Personalisation hooks: What variables or research points are they using to feel personal?
- Value proposition: How quickly do they communicate the benefit? What framing do they use?
- Social proof: Are they using case studies, numbers, name drops, or credibility signals?
- CTA style: What is the ask? How soft or direct is it? Single CTA or multiple?
- Email length: How many words/lines? What is the ideal length pattern?
- Follow-up sequences: If visible, how many follow-ups and what angle does each take?
- Subject lines: What patterns are the subject lines using?

## My Details
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- My offer: [YOUR OFFER]
- Who I am reaching out to: [e.g. agency owners, SaaS founders, local businesses]

## Output Format
1. Pattern breakdown for each element above with specific examples from the page
2. The 3 strongest templates ranked by likely conversion potential
3. A custom cold email template for my business using the best patterns identified
4. A 3-email follow-up sequence using the follow-up structures found
5. Red flags - any templates that would likely get ignored or marked as spam
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A detailed pattern breakdown of every cold email element on the page
- The top 3 templates ranked with reasoning for why they work
- A custom cold email written for your specific niche and offer
- A ready-to-use 3-email follow-up sequence
- Spam risk warnings so you know which approaches to avoid

## Pro Tips
- Scrape templates from multiple sources and compare - the overlapping patterns are the proven ones
- Always personalise beyond just the first name - reference something specific about the prospect's business
- Test your custom template on 50 prospects before tweaking - small samples give unreliable data
