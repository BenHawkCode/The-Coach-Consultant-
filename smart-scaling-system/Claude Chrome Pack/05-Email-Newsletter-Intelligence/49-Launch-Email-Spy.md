# Prompt 49: Launch Email Spy

**Category:** Email & Newsletter Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Competitor launch email in browser
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Reverse-engineers a competitor's launch email to expose exactly how they build urgency, frame their offer, use scarcity, deploy social proof, and structure their CTA. You get the complete playbook behind their email-to-sales flow so you can apply the same psychology to your own launches.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Launch Email Spy

Reverse-engineer this launch email completely. I want to understand every persuasion tactic, structural choice, and conversion technique being used - then apply the best of it to my own launches.

## Analyse These Elements
- Urgency tactics: Deadlines, countdown language, "last chance" framing - what are they using and how?
- Offer framing: How is the offer presented? Price anchoring, value stacking, bonuses, guarantee?
- Scarcity signals: Limited spots, closing soon, price increases - real or manufactured?
- Social proof: Testimonials, case studies, numbers, name drops, community size?
- CTA structure: How many CTAs? Where are they placed? What language and design?
- Email-to-sales flow: Where does the CTA lead? What is the next step in their funnel?
- Emotional triggers: Fear of missing out, aspiration, pain avoidance, belonging?
- Objection handling: Do they address common objections within the email?
- Design choices: Layout, formatting, images, colours - how do these support the message?

## My Details
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- My offer: [YOUR OFFER]
- My next launch: [e.g. course launch / programme enrollment / service promotion]

## Output Format
1. Tactic-by-tactic breakdown with specific examples from the email
2. The persuasion sequence mapped in order (how the email builds to the sale)
3. Effectiveness rating for each tactic (strong / moderate / weak)
4. A launch email template for my offer using the strongest tactics identified
5. What they did poorly or could improve
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Every persuasion tactic identified with specific examples from the email
- The full psychological sequence mapped from opening line to final CTA
- Effectiveness ratings so you know which tactics are worth stealing
- A custom launch email template built for your specific offer
- Weaknesses in their approach you can avoid in your own launches
- A clear understanding of how the email feeds into their wider sales funnel

## Pro Tips
- Collect launch emails from 5+ competitors over time to build a master launch playbook
- Pay closest attention to the first 3 lines and the CTA - those determine open-to-click conversion
- Screenshot the email before analysing - launch emails often expire or get updated
