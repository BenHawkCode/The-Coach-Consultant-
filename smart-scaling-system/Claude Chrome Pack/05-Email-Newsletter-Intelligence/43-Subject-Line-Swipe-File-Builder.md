# Prompt 43: Subject Line Swipe File Builder

**Category:** Email & Newsletter Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Your email platform sent folder
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Scans your sent emails to identify which subject lines performed best, extracts the patterns behind them, turns those patterns into reusable formulas, and generates 20 fresh subject lines you can use immediately. Your own data becomes your best copywriting resource.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Subject Line Swipe File Builder

Analyse the subject lines visible on this page from my sent emails. I want to turn my own email history into a swipe file I can reuse forever.

## What I Need
- Pattern identification: Group subject lines by type (question, statement, curiosity gap, urgency, personalisation, number-based, etc.)
- Performance correlation: Based on any visible open rates or engagement data, which types are performing best?
- Winning formulas: Extract 5-7 reusable subject line templates from the best performers
- New subject lines: Generate 20 new subject lines using my winning formulas, tailored to my niche

## My Details
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- My offer: [YOUR OFFER]
- Topics I email about: [e.g. marketing tips, business growth, mindset, case studies]

## Output Format
1. Subject line audit: group every visible subject line by pattern type
2. Performance ranking: best to worst pattern types based on available data
3. 5-7 reusable formulas written as fill-in-the-blank templates
4. 20 new subject lines organised by formula type
5. 5 subject line mistakes to stop making based on the weakest performers
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Every subject line categorised by pattern type for easy reference
- A clear ranking of which subject line styles work best for your audience
- Reusable fill-in-the-blank formulas you can apply to any email topic
- 20 ready-to-use subject lines tailored to your niche and offer
- Common mistakes identified from your lowest performers
- A permanent swipe file built from your own proven data

## Pro Tips
- Run this every quarter as your sent folder grows - your formulas will get sharper over time
- Test the generated subject lines in A/B splits to validate the formulas
- Keep a separate document of your top 10 all-time subject lines and update it regularly
