# Prompt 47: Sales Email Personaliser

**Category:** Email & Newsletter Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Prospect's website or LinkedIn profile
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Medium

## What This Does
Writes a personalised sales email based on what is visible on a prospect's website or LinkedIn profile. Instead of sending generic outreach, you get an email that references their specific challenges, tailors your offer to their situation, and includes relevant proof points. Personalisation at scale without the hours of research.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
# Personalised Sales Email Writer

Analyse this page and write a personalised sales email to this prospect. The email must feel like I have genuinely researched their business - not like a template with their name swapped in.

## Research This Page For
- Their business model: What do they sell, who do they serve?
- Visible challenges: What problems can you spot from their messaging, positioning, or gaps?
- Recent activity: Any new launches, blog posts, job listings, or changes that signal priorities?
- Language they use: Mirror their terminology and tone in the email
- Specific praise points: One genuine, specific thing I can compliment that shows I paid attention

## My Details
- My brand: [YOUR BRAND]
- My offer: [YOUR OFFER]
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- Key result I deliver: [e.g. "help business owners generate 20+ leads per month through content systems"]
- Relevant proof point: [e.g. "helped 40+ business owners build automated content pipelines"]

## Email Requirements
- Subject line: personalised, curiosity-driven, under 8 words
- Opening line: reference something specific from their page - no generic "I came across your profile"
- Bridge: connect their situation to the problem I solve in 2-3 sentences
- Offer: position my service as the solution to their specific challenge
- Social proof: include my proof point naturally, not as a boast
- CTA: soft ask - suggest a quick call or reply, not a hard sell
- Length: under 150 words total

## Output Format
1. The personalised sales email ready to send
2. Subject line with 2 alternatives
3. The 3 personalisation hooks used and why they were chosen
4. A suggested follow-up email for 3 days later if no reply
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- A fully personalised sales email referencing specific details from their page
- Three subject line options ranked by likely open rate
- Explanation of the personalisation hooks so you can learn the technique
- A follow-up email ready to send if they do not reply
- An email that reads like genuine outreach, not a mass blast
- Proper length and structure optimised for cold email response rates

## Pro Tips
- Use this on their website for business-level personalisation and their LinkedIn for personal-level personalisation
- Send within 24 hours of any visible activity (new post, launch, hiring) for maximum relevance
- Always check the email reads naturally before sending - over-personalisation can feel creepy
