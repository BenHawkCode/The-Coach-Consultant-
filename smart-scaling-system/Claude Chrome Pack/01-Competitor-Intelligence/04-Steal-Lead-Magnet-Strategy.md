# Prompt 04: Steal Their Lead Magnet Strategy

**Category:** Competitor Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Competitor's lead magnet or opt-in page
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Analyses a competitor's entire lead magnet strategy - the opt-in offer, how they frame value, what they promise, and how they capture leads. Gives you the blueprint to build a higher-converting lead magnet that outperforms theirs by addressing what they missed.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
Analyse this lead magnet / opt-in page in full. I want to understand their entire lead generation strategy so I can build something better.

# My Context
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My brand: [YOUR BRAND]
- My target audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- My current lead magnet (if any): [YOUR LEAD MAGNET or "none yet"]

# Lead Magnet Analysis

## 1. The Offer
- What exactly are they giving away? (PDF, video, quiz, template, checklist, mini-course)
- What is the headline promise?
- What specific outcome or result does the lead magnet promise?
- How do they frame the perceived value?

## 2. Opt-in Page Breakdown
- What is the main headline and sub-headline?
- What bullet points or benefit statements do they use?
- How many form fields are required? (name only, name + email, more?)
- What does the CTA button say?
- Is there a preview or mockup of the deliverable?

## 3. Persuasion Elements
- What social proof is on this page (download count, testimonials, logos)?
- Is there urgency or scarcity? (limited time, countdown, spots remaining)
- What objections do they pre-handle on this page?
- How do they reduce friction and risk?

## 4. Funnel Context
- Where does this lead magnet sit in their funnel? (top of funnel, middle, bridge to paid)
- What is the likely next step after someone opts in? (email sequence, upsell, booking page)
- How does the lead magnet topic connect to their paid offer?

## 5. Strengths & Weaknesses
- What is this page doing well that I should learn from?
- Where is it weak - vague promises, poor design, missing social proof, too many fields?
- What would make someone NOT opt in?

## 6. How I Can Beat This
- What lead magnet could [YOUR BRAND] create that delivers more specific value?
- What format would work better for [YOUR AUDIENCE]?
- What headline angle would outperform theirs?
- Give me 3 lead magnet ideas that would directly compete with and outperform this one.

Be specific and reference exact text from the page.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Full breakdown of the lead magnet offer and how it is positioned
- Opt-in page structure analysis with conversion element inventory
- Persuasion and friction assessment
- Funnel mapping showing where the lead magnet fits in their strategy
- Weaknesses and missed opportunities
- Three specific lead magnet ideas tailored to beat their approach

## Pro Tips
- Actually opt in to their lead magnet so you can analyse the follow-up email sequence too
- Check if they promote the same lead magnet across social media - consistency signals it is working
- The best lead magnets solve a specific problem that naturally leads to the paid offer - look for that bridge
