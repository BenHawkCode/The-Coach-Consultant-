# Prompt 01: Scrape a Competitor's Entire Website

**Category:** Competitor Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Competitor's homepage
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Pulls apart a competitor's entire homepage to extract their positioning, core offers, messaging angles, calls-to-action, and social proof strategy. Gives you a full intelligence briefing you can use to sharpen your own website and find gaps in their approach.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
Analyse this competitor's homepage in full. I want a complete intelligence briefing covering every element on this page.

# My Context
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My brand: [YOUR BRAND]
- My primary offer: [YOUR OFFER]

# What To Extract

## 1. Positioning & Messaging
- What is their core headline and sub-headline?
- What transformation or outcome are they promising?
- Who exactly are they speaking to? (demographics, pain points, desires)
- What language patterns and power words do they repeat?

## 2. Offer Structure
- What products or services are visible on this page?
- How do they frame the value of each offer?
- What's the primary offer vs secondary offers?

## 3. Calls-to-Action
- List every CTA on the page (button text, placement, colour)
- What is the primary conversion action they want visitors to take?
- How many times does the main CTA appear?

## 4. Social Proof & Trust
- What testimonials, logos, stats, or case studies are shown?
- Where is social proof placed relative to CTAs?
- What credibility signals do they use (certifications, media mentions, client counts)?

## 5. Gaps & Opportunities
- What objections are they NOT addressing?
- What audience segments are they ignoring?
- Where is their messaging weak or generic?
- How could [YOUR BRAND] position differently to win?

Format the output with clear headers and bullet points. Be specific - quote exact text from the page where relevant.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Full breakdown of their headline hierarchy and positioning statement
- Complete list of offers, CTAs, and conversion mechanisms
- Social proof inventory with placement analysis
- Language and messaging patterns they rely on
- Specific gaps and weaknesses you can exploit
- Actionable positioning recommendations for your own brand

## Pro Tips
- Run this on your top 3 competitors and compare the outputs side by side
- Pay close attention to the "Gaps & Opportunities" section - that is where your differentiation lives
- Save the output and re-run every quarter to track how competitors evolve their messaging
