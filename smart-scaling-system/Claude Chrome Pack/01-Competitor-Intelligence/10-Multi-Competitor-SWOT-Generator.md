# Prompt 10: Multi-Competitor SWOT Generator

**Category:** Competitor Intelligence
**Where to Use:** After analysing 2-3 competitors using the prompts above
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Medium

## What This Does
Takes all the intelligence you have gathered from analysing multiple competitors and synthesises it into a SWOT analysis for your own business. Turns raw competitor data into a strategic action plan showing exactly where you are strong, where you are vulnerable, and where the biggest opportunities sit.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
I have analysed several competitors in my niche. Using everything visible on this page plus the competitor intelligence I paste below, generate a comprehensive SWOT analysis for MY business.

# My Business Context
- My brand: [YOUR BRAND]
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My target audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- My primary offer: [YOUR OFFER]
- My website: [YOUR WEBSITE]
- My price point: [YOUR PRICE]

# Competitor Intelligence
[PASTE YOUR KEY FINDINGS FROM PROMPTS 01-09 HERE - competitor names, their positioning, pricing, strengths, weaknesses, messaging gaps you identified]

# SWOT Analysis

## 1. STRENGTHS - Where [YOUR BRAND] Already Wins
- What do I do better than the competitors I analysed?
- Where is my positioning, offer, or messaging stronger?
- What unique advantages do I have (experience, methodology, audience trust, pricing)?
- What are my competitors consistently weak at that I am strong at?

## 2. WEAKNESSES - Where I Am Vulnerable
- Where are competitors outperforming me?
- What do they have that I lack (social proof, content volume, brand recognition, features)?
- Where is my messaging, positioning, or offer weaker than theirs?
- What gaps in my business could a competitor exploit?

## 3. OPPORTUNITIES - Gaps I Can Exploit Right Now
- What audience segments are ALL competitors ignoring?
- What content topics, formats, or channels are underserved in this market?
- What pricing positions are unclaimed?
- What messaging angles are no one using?
- What customer pain points are competitors failing to address?

## 4. THREATS - Risks to Watch
- Which competitors are growing fastest and why?
- What market shifts could undermine my current positioning?
- Where could a new entrant easily overtake me?
- What competitor moves should I be monitoring?

## 5. Strategic Action Plan
Based on this SWOT, give me:
- The top 3 actions I should take THIS WEEK to capitalise on opportunities
- The top 3 weaknesses I should fix THIS MONTH
- The single biggest strategic move that would put [YOUR BRAND] ahead of all analysed competitors
- A 90-day priority roadmap (bullet points, not a full plan)

Be specific and actionable. No generic advice. Every recommendation should reference a specific finding from the competitor analysis.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Strengths analysis showing where you already outperform competitors
- Honest weakness assessment identifying your vulnerabilities
- Opportunity map with specific gaps you can exploit immediately
- Threat radar highlighting competitors and market shifts to monitor
- Prioritised action plan with this-week, this-month, and 90-day recommendations
- One strategic move that would create the biggest competitive advantage

## Pro Tips
- This prompt works best when you paste in findings from at least 2-3 competitors - the more data, the sharper the analysis
- Run this quarterly as you gather fresh competitor intelligence to keep your strategy current
- Share the 90-day roadmap with your team or accountability partner to turn analysis into execution
