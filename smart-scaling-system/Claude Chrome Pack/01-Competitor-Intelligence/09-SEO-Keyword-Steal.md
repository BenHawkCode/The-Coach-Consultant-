# Prompt 09: SEO Keyword Steal

**Category:** Competitor Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Competitor's blog post or key page
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Extracts every keyword signal from a competitor's page - title tags, headers, repeated phrases, meta content, and topic clusters. Builds you a keyword hit list you can target with your own content to compete for the same search traffic they are already capturing.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
Extract every SEO keyword signal from this page. I want a complete keyword intelligence report I can use to compete for the same search traffic.

# My Context
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My brand: [YOUR BRAND]
- My website: [YOUR WEBSITE]

# SEO Keyword Extraction

## 1. Page Title & Meta
- What is the exact page title (shown in the browser tab)?
- What is the H1 heading?
- Are the page title and H1 different? What does that suggest about their SEO strategy?

## 2. Header Hierarchy
- List every H2 and H3 heading on the page in order
- What keyword themes emerge from the header structure?
- Are headers optimised for search (keyword-rich) or for readability?

## 3. Keyword Frequency
- What words and phrases appear 3+ times on this page?
- What 2-3 word phrases (long-tail keywords) are repeated?
- What semantic variations of the same keyword do they use?

## 4. Content Signals
- What is the approximate word count?
- How many images are there and do they have descriptive alt text visible?
- Are there internal links? What pages do they link to and with what anchor text?
- Are there external links? What authority sites do they reference?

## 5. Topic Cluster Mapping
- What is the primary keyword this page is targeting?
- What related subtopics does this page cover?
- What cluster of related content would this page belong to?
- What supporting pages would strengthen this topic cluster?

## 6. Keyword Hit List for [YOUR BRAND]
- List the top 10 keywords this page is targeting, ranked by likely search volume
- For each keyword, rate the competition level: Low / Medium / High
- Which keywords could [YOUR BRAND] realistically rank for?
- Give me 5 long-tail keyword variations I could target that this page does not fully cover
- Suggest a content outline for the highest-opportunity keyword

Format the keyword hit list as a table with columns: Keyword | Estimated Competition | Opportunity Level | Content Angle.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Page title and header hierarchy with keyword patterns
- Keyword frequency analysis showing their primary targets
- Internal and external linking strategy breakdown
- Topic cluster map showing how this content fits their broader SEO strategy
- Ranked keyword hit list with competition and opportunity ratings
- Five long-tail keyword opportunities with a content outline for the best one

## Pro Tips
- Run this on their top 5-10 blog posts to build a comprehensive keyword map of their entire SEO strategy
- Focus on long-tail keywords with lower competition - those are your quickest wins
- Use the content outline output as a brief for your own blog post or article targeting that keyword
