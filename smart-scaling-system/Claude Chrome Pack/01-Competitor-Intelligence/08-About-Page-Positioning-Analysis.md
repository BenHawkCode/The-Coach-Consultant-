# Prompt 08: About Page Positioning Analysis

**Category:** Competitor Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Competitor's about page
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Decodes how a competitor positions their brand through their about page - the story they tell, the authority they claim, how they differentiate, and what values they lead with. Gives you the insight to craft a stronger brand narrative that stands apart from theirs.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
Analyse this competitor's about page in full. I want to understand exactly how they position their brand, build authority, and differentiate themselves - so I can do it better.

# My Context
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My brand: [YOUR BRAND]
- My audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]

# About Page Positioning Analysis

## 1. Brand Story Arc
- What origin story do they tell? (struggle, discovery, mission)
- What is the narrative structure? (hero's journey, problem-solution, credentials-first)
- What emotions are they trying to trigger (inspiration, trust, relatability, aspiration)?
- Is the story founder-led, brand-led, or mission-led?

## 2. Authority & Credentials
- What qualifications, certifications, or experience do they claim?
- What numbers do they use (years in business, clients served, revenue generated)?
- Do they mention media features, awards, or partnerships?
- How do they establish expertise without just listing credentials?

## 3. Team & Structure
- Is this a personal brand or a company brand?
- How many team members are shown? What roles?
- How do they present the team (professional headshots, casual photos, bios)?
- What does the team structure signal about their scale and capability?

## 4. Values & Mission
- What values do they explicitly state?
- What implicit values come through in their language and imagery?
- Do they have a clear mission statement? What is it?
- How do they align their values with what [YOUR AUDIENCE] cares about?

## 5. Differentiation Claims
- How do they say they are different from others in [YOUR NICHE]?
- What unique methodology, framework, or approach do they claim?
- Is their differentiation genuine or generic ("we care more", "we go the extra mile")?
- What positioning gap exists that they are not filling?

## 6. Positioning Lessons for [YOUR BRAND]
- What is this about page doing well that I should learn from?
- Where is it generic, weak, or unconvincing?
- What story elements or authority signals are they missing?
- How should [YOUR BRAND] position differently to stand out from this competitor?
- What specific claims or proof points would make my about page stronger than theirs?

Quote exact language from the page to support your analysis.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Brand story arc breakdown with narrative structure identified
- Full authority and credentials inventory
- Team structure and brand positioning analysis
- Values and mission assessment
- Differentiation claim evaluation - genuine vs generic
- Specific recommendations for making your own about page stronger

## Pro Tips
- Your about page is one of the most visited pages on your site - most people check it before buying, so this analysis directly impacts conversions
- Look for what their story does NOT include - that is often where your own story can stand out
- If their differentiation claims are generic, that is your biggest opportunity - get specific about what makes you different
