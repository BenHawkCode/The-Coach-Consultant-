# Prompt 07: Blog & Content Strategy Scrape

**Category:** Competitor Intelligence
**Where to Use:** Competitor's blog index or content hub page
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Analyses a competitor's entire content strategy from their blog - topics, posting frequency, content pillars, SEO patterns, and engagement signals. Reveals what content is working for them and where the gaps are that you can fill with your own content.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
Analyse this competitor's blog/content index page. I want a full breakdown of their content strategy so I can identify what is working, what is missing, and where I can outperform them.

# My Context
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My brand: [YOUR BRAND]
- My audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- My content channels: [YOUR CHANNELS e.g. blog, YouTube, Instagram, LinkedIn]

# Content Strategy Analysis

## 1. Content Inventory
- How many posts/articles are visible on this page?
- What are the most recent 10 post titles?
- What is the approximate posting frequency (weekly, fortnightly, monthly, irregular)?
- What date range does the visible content cover?

## 2. Content Pillars & Topics
- Group the content into 4-6 topic categories/pillars
- Which pillar has the most content?
- Which topics are they clearly prioritising?
- What topics are suspiciously absent that [YOUR AUDIENCE] would care about?

## 3. SEO Patterns
- What keyword patterns appear in their titles?
- Are they using how-to, listicle, comparison, or guide formats?
- Do titles follow a consistent formula? What is it?
- What long-tail keywords are they clearly targeting?

## 4. Content Quality Signals
- How long do the posts appear to be (short, medium, in-depth)?
- Do they include images, graphics, videos, or embedded media?
- Is there original research, data, or unique frameworks?
- Do they have author bios, publish dates, and content categories?

## 5. Engagement & Distribution
- Are there visible comment counts, share buttons, or engagement metrics?
- Do they promote email opt-ins within the content?
- Is there internal linking between posts?
- Do any posts appear to be gated or lead into paid content?

## 6. Gaps & Opportunities for [YOUR BRAND]
- What topics in [YOUR NICHE] are they completely ignoring?
- What content formats are they not using that would work for [YOUR AUDIENCE]?
- What existing posts could you write a better, more comprehensive version of?
- Give me 10 specific content ideas that would outrank or outperform their content based on these gaps.

List all findings with specific examples and post titles from the page.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Full content inventory with posting frequency analysis
- Content pillar mapping showing their strategic focus
- SEO pattern analysis with keyword targeting insights
- Content quality and format assessment
- Engagement and distribution strategy breakdown
- Ten specific content ideas tailored to exploit their gaps

## Pro Tips
- Sort their blog by most popular or most commented if the option exists - that tells you what actually resonates with their audience
- Cross-reference their blog topics with their social media to see which content they repurpose (and which they do not)
- The 10 content ideas output is a ready-made content calendar - prioritise the ones targeting gaps they are not covering
