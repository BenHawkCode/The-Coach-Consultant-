# Prompt 17: YouTube Channel Analyser

**Category:** Social Media Hacking
**Where to Use:** A competitor's YouTube channel page (Videos tab)
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Analyses a YouTube channel's entire visible strategy - content themes, title patterns, thumbnail approaches, posting frequency, and engagement signals. Reveals what's working for them and identifies content gaps you can fill in your own niche.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
You are looking at a YouTube channel page. Analyse everything visible and produce a full channel intelligence report.

# Channel Overview
- What is this channel's core positioning and who do they serve?
- How many subscribers and what does their growth trajectory look like?
- What's their approximate posting frequency?

# Content Theme Mapping
- Identify the 4-6 main content pillars/themes across their videos
- Which themes get the most views relative to their average?
- Which themes consistently underperform?
- Are there any emerging themes they've recently started testing?

# Title Pattern Analysis
- List the 5 highest-viewed video titles
- What title formulas do they repeat? Extract the patterns:
  - Do they use numbers, questions, "How to", bold claims?
  - What's the average title length?
  - Do they use power words and which ones appear most?
- Create 5 title formula templates based on their best performers

# Thumbnail Pattern Analysis
- What visual patterns appear across their thumbnails?
- Do they use faces, text overlays, before/after, or other techniques?
- Which thumbnail styles correlate with higher view counts?
- What colours, fonts, or graphic elements are consistent?

# Engagement Signals
- Which videos have the highest views relative to subscriber count?
- Are there videos with unusually high engagement (comments/likes to views)?
- What topics drive the most discussion in their community?

# Content Gaps & Opportunities
- What topics should this channel cover but hasn't?
- What questions is their audience likely asking that aren't addressed?
- What content gaps exist that I could fill for [YOUR NICHE] targeting [YOUR AUDIENCE]?

# Steal-Worthy Strategy
List the top 5 strategic takeaways I should apply to my own YouTube channel, with specific examples of how I'd adapt each one for [YOUR NICHE].
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Full content pillar map with performance ratings for each theme
- Title formula templates extracted from their best-performing videos
- Thumbnail pattern analysis with actionable design insights
- Engagement signal breakdown showing what their audience responds to most
- Content gap opportunities you can target immediately
- 5 strategic takeaways adapted for your niche

## Pro Tips
- Analyse 3-5 channels in your niche to find content themes that perform well across all of them
- Sort by "Most Popular" if available - that's where the real intelligence lives
- Their worst-performing videos are just as valuable as their best - they show you what to avoid
