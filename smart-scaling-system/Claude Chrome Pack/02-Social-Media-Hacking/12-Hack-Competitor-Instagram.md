# Prompt 12: Hack Competitor Instagram

**Category:** Social Media Hacking
**Where to Use:** A competitor's Instagram profile page
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Performs a deep competitor analysis on any Instagram profile in your space. Extracts their content strategy, posting patterns, engagement tactics, hook formulas, and growth signals - giving you a blueprint of what's working for them that you can adapt for your own brand.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
You are looking at a competitor's Instagram profile in my niche. Run a full competitive intelligence audit on everything visible.

# Profile Positioning
- What is their core positioning? Who do they serve and what do they promise?
- How strong is their bio as a conversion tool? What makes it effective or weak?
- What authority signals are they using (follower count, verification, social proof)?

# Content Strategy Breakdown
- What content types do they use most (reels, carousels, single images)?
- What are their 3-5 core content pillars/themes?
- What's their approximate posting frequency?
- Do they have a visible content pattern or series they run regularly?

# Hook & Copy Analysis
- List the 5 strongest hooks/opening lines visible in their recent posts
- What hook patterns do they repeat (questions, bold claims, stories, stats)?
- How do they structure their captions (length, formatting, tone)?
- What CTA styles do they use and how often?

# Engagement Intelligence
- Which of their recent posts got the highest engagement? What do those share?
- What types of comments are they getting (genuine engagement vs generic)?
- How do they respond to comments - and does it drive more conversation?

# Growth Signals
- Are they growing, plateauing, or declining based on visible signals?
- What tactics are they using to drive growth (collaborations, hashtags, trends)?
- What's their biggest strategic weakness I could exploit?

# Steal-Worthy Tactics
List the top 5 specific things this competitor does well that I should adapt for my own profile. For each one, explain HOW I'd adapt it for [YOUR NICHE] targeting [YOUR AUDIENCE].
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Complete breakdown of their positioning and brand promise
- Content pillar map showing exactly what topics they cover
- Their best-performing hook formulas you can adapt
- Engagement pattern analysis revealing what their audience responds to
- Strategic weaknesses you can exploit in your own content
- 5 specific tactics to steal and adapt for your brand

## Pro Tips
- Run this on 3-5 competitors to spot patterns across your entire niche
- Pay special attention to hooks that get high engagement - those formulas work for a reason
- Look for content gaps none of your competitors are covering - that's your opportunity
