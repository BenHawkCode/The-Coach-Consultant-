# Prompt 13: Merge Profile Assessments

**Category:** Social Media Hacking
**Where to Use:** Same Claude Chrome session after running Prompts 11 and 12
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Combines the results from your own profile audit (Prompt 11) and competitor audit (Prompt 12) into a direct gap analysis. Shows exactly where you're behind, where you're ahead, and gives you a specific action plan to close the gaps that matter most.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
I've just run two profile audits in this conversation. Now combine both assessments into a competitive gap analysis.

If you don't have both audits in this conversation, I'll paste them below:

[PASTE YOUR PROFILE AUDIT RESULTS FROM PROMPT 11 HERE - delete this line if already in the conversation]

[PASTE COMPETITOR AUDIT RESULTS FROM PROMPT 12 HERE - delete this line if already in the conversation]

# Gap Analysis

## Positioning Comparison
- Compare our bio/positioning side by side. Where is theirs stronger? Where is mine stronger?
- What positioning angle are they missing that I could own?

## Content Strategy Gaps
- What content types or pillars are they using that I'm not?
- What am I doing that they're not (my unique advantages)?
- Where is my content mix weaker and what specific shifts should I make?

## Engagement Gap
- Compare engagement quality between both profiles
- What engagement tactics are they using that I should adopt?
- What's driving their best engagement that I'm currently missing?

## Hook & Copy Comparison
- How do my hooks compare to theirs in terms of strength and variety?
- What hook formulas should I start using based on what works for them?
- Where is my copy stronger and how do I lean into that advantage?

## Visual & Brand Comparison
- How does my visual consistency compare to theirs?
- What visual elements should I adopt or improve?

# 30-Day Action Plan
Based on this gap analysis, create a prioritised 30-day action plan with:
- Week 1: Quick wins I can implement immediately
- Week 2: Content strategy shifts to test
- Week 3: Engagement and growth tactic changes
- Week 4: Review and optimise based on results

Be brutally honest about where I'm losing. I want the truth, not encouragement.

My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
My offer: [YOUR OFFER]
My website: [YOUR WEBSITE]
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Side-by-side positioning comparison with clear winner in each area
- Content strategy gaps mapped with specific fixes
- Engagement tactic differences and what to adopt
- Hook and copy quality comparison with formulas to steal
- A structured 30-day action plan prioritised by impact
- Honest assessment of where you're losing and how to close the gap

## Pro Tips
- Run this against your top 3 competitors separately - patterns will emerge across all of them
- Focus on the gaps where the fix is easy but the impact is high - those are your quick wins
- Revisit this analysis monthly to track whether you're actually closing the gaps
