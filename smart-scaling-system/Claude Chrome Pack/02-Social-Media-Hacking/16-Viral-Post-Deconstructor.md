# Prompt 16: Viral Post Deconstructor

**Category:** Social Media Hacking
**Where to Use:** Any viral social media post (high engagement relative to account size)
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Breaks down exactly why a specific post went viral - the structural elements, psychological triggers, and tactical choices that drove outsized engagement. Gives you a replicable framework so you can engineer similar results with your own content.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
You are looking at a social media post that got significantly higher engagement than typical. Deconstruct exactly why this post performed so well.

# Hook Deconstruction
- What is the exact hook/opening line?
- What psychological trigger does it activate (curiosity, fear, aspiration, controversy, relatability)?
- Why does it stop the scroll? Be specific about the mechanism.
- Rate the hook out of 10 and explain what makes it effective

# Content Structure Analysis
- Map out the post structure section by section (hook, body, CTA)
- What formatting techniques are used (line breaks, spacing, emojis, caps)?
- How long is the post and does the length serve the content?
- What's the reading rhythm - does it pull you through or does it drag?

# Emotional Architecture
- What emotion does the hook trigger?
- Does the emotional tone shift throughout the post? Map the emotional journey.
- What makes someone feel compelled to comment, share, or save this?
- Which specific lines would trigger the most reactions?

# CTA & Engagement Mechanics
- What's the call to action (explicit or implicit)?
- Does the post use engagement bait techniques? Which ones?
- What about this post makes people want to tag someone or share it?
- Are there comment triggers embedded in the content?

# Platform-Specific Tactics
- What platform-specific features or algorithm signals is this post leveraging?
- Is the timing/topic riding a current trend or conversation?

# Replication Framework
Create a fill-in-the-blank template based on this post's structure that I can use to write a similar post about [YOUR NICHE] for [YOUR AUDIENCE]. Include:
- The hook formula
- The body structure
- The emotional arc
- The CTA approach
- One example post using this framework for my niche
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Hook breakdown with the specific psychological mechanism at play
- Full structural map of the post from opening to CTA
- Emotional architecture analysis showing why people engaged
- Engagement mechanic identification (what drove comments, shares, saves)
- A reusable template framework based on the post's structure
- One ready-to-adapt example post for your niche

## Pro Tips
- Deconstruct 10 viral posts in your niche and you'll spot 3-4 repeating formulas
- Save the replication frameworks in a swipe file - they compound over time
- Pay more attention to posts that went viral from small accounts - those relied on structure, not audience size
