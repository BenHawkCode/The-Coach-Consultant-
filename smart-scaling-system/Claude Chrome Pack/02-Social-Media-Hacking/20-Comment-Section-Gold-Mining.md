# Prompt 20: Comment Section Gold Mining

**Category:** Social Media Hacking
**Where to Use:** Any social media post with 50+ comments
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Turns any busy comment section into a business intelligence goldmine. Extracts objections, desires, language patterns, content ideas, and even potential lead opportunities from the raw, unfiltered things people say when they're reacting to content in your niche.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
You are looking at a social media post with a large comment section. Analyse every visible comment and extract business intelligence I can use.

# Objection Mining
- List every objection, doubt, or pushback visible in the comments
- Categorise them: price objections, trust objections, "this won't work for me" objections, timing objections
- Note the exact language used for each objection - I want their words, not paraphrased versions
- Which objections appear most frequently?

# Desire & Want Extraction
- What are people saying they want, need, or wish existed?
- What results or outcomes are they asking about?
- What are the "I need this" or "where can I find this" type comments?
- What gaps between their current situation and desired outcome are they revealing?

# Language Pattern Bank
- Extract 10-15 specific phrases commenters use to describe their problems
- Extract 10-15 phrases they use to describe their desired outcomes
- What emotional words come up repeatedly (frustrated, overwhelmed, stuck, excited)?
- Build me a copy bank of real audience language I can use in my marketing

# Content Ideas
- What questions are people asking in the comments that deserve their own post?
- What debates or disagreements are happening that I could create content around?
- What misconceptions are visible that I could address in educational content?
- List 10 specific content ideas pulled directly from these comments

# Lead Opportunity Signals
- Are there comments from people who are clearly in a buying mindset?
- Who is asking for help, recommendations, or solutions?
- What signals indicate someone is ready to invest in solving this problem?
- How could I position [YOUR OFFER] to speak directly to these people?

# Summary Intelligence Report
Give me a one-page summary of:
- The #1 objection I need to address in my marketing
- The #1 desire I should lead with in my messaging
- The 5 most powerful phrases to steal for my copy
- The 3 best content ideas from this comment section

My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
My audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
My offer: [YOUR OFFER]
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Categorised objection list with exact audience language for each
- Desire and want extraction showing what people are actively seeking
- 20-30 phrase copy bank pulled from real audience language
- 10 content ideas sourced directly from audience conversations
- Lead opportunity signals showing buying intent patterns
- One-page summary with the highest-impact takeaways

## Pro Tips
- The best comment sections to mine are on controversial or opinion-based posts - people reveal more when they're reacting emotionally
- Run this on your own high-engagement posts too - your audience's comments are the best market research you'll ever get
- Use the objection language directly in your FAQ sections and sales pages - when people see their exact concern addressed, trust goes up instantly
