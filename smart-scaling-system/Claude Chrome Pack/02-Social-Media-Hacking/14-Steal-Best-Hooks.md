# Prompt 14: Steal Best Hooks

**Category:** Social Media Hacking
**Where to Use:** A competitor's Instagram or LinkedIn feed
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Extracts and categorises every high-performing hook visible on a competitor's feed. Breaks down the psychological triggers, structural patterns, and formulas behind their best-performing opening lines - then generates adapted versions you can use immediately for your own content.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
You are looking at a social media feed. Analyse every visible post and extract a complete hook intelligence report.

# Hook Extraction
List every opening line/hook visible on this page. For each one, include:
- The exact hook text
- Estimated engagement level (high/medium/low based on visible likes/comments)
- The hook category it falls into (see categories below)

# Hook Categories
Classify each hook into one or more of these categories:
- Bold claim or hot take
- Question that triggers curiosity
- Story opener or pattern interrupt
- Stat or data point
- "How to" or educational
- Contrarian or myth-busting
- Fear/pain agitation
- Aspirational or result-based

# Pattern Analysis
- Which hook categories appear most often on this feed?
- Which hook categories get the highest engagement?
- What's the average hook length (short punchy vs longer setup)?
- Do they use emojis, caps, or formatting tricks in hooks?
- What emotional triggers do the top 5 hooks tap into?

# Hook Formulas
Extract the top 5 repeatable hook FORMULAS from this feed. Write each as a fill-in-the-blank template, like:
"[Unexpected truth] about [topic] that [audience] don't want to hear"

# Adapted Hooks for My Brand
Take the top 10 strongest hooks from this feed and rewrite each one adapted for:
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- My offer: [YOUR OFFER]

Make them sound natural, not like copies. Change the angle, topic, or framing while keeping the structural formula that made the original work.
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Complete catalogue of every visible hook with engagement ratings
- Hook category breakdown showing what types dominate
- Pattern analysis revealing the psychological triggers behind top performers
- 5 reusable hook formulas you can apply to any topic
- 10 ready-to-use hooks adapted specifically for your brand and niche

## Pro Tips
- Run this on 5 different competitors and combine the hook formulas into a master swipe file
- The hook formulas are more valuable than the individual hooks - those patterns work across any topic
- Test the adapted hooks against your current hook style to see which format your audience prefers
