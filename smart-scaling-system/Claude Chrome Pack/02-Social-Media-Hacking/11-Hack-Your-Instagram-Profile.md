# Prompt 11: Hack Your Instagram Profile

**Category:** Social Media Hacking
**Where to Use:** Your own Instagram profile page
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Runs a full audit of your Instagram profile - bio, content mix, visual consistency, engagement patterns, and overall positioning. You get a clear picture of what's working, what's falling flat, and what to change immediately to convert more profile visitors into followers and buyers.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
You are looking at my Instagram profile. Run a full profile audit using everything visible on this page.

# Bio & First Impression Audit
- Rate the bio out of 10 for clarity, authority, and conversion potential
- Does it clearly state WHO I help, WHAT I help them with, and HOW to take action?
- Is the CTA in the bio strong enough to drive clicks?
- Profile picture - does it look professional and appropriate for [YOUR NICHE]?

# Content Mix Analysis
- What percentage split am I running across content types (reels, carousels, single images, stories highlights)?
- What topics/themes appear most frequently?
- Which content type appears to get the most engagement based on visible likes/comments?
- Am I missing any content types that would strengthen the profile?

# Visual Consistency Check
- Rate the grid aesthetic out of 10
- Is there a consistent colour palette, font usage, or visual style?
- Do the first 9 posts create a strong enough impression to make someone follow?

# Engagement Pattern Analysis
- Based on visible metrics, which posts are outperforming?
- What patterns do the top-performing posts share (format, topic, hook style)?
- Are there posts that clearly underperformed? What do they have in common?

# Action Plan
Give me a prioritised list of 5 specific changes I should make to this profile in the next 7 days to increase follower conversion rate. Be direct - tell me what's weak and what to fix.

My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
My target audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
My main offer: [YOUR OFFER]
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Bio effectiveness score with specific rewrite suggestions
- Content mix breakdown showing gaps and imbalances
- Visual consistency rating with actionable fixes
- Top-performing post patterns you should double down on
- Underperforming content patterns to stop or rework
- Prioritised 7-day action plan for immediate improvements

## Pro Tips
- Run this every 30 days to track whether changes are actually improving performance
- Screenshot the results and compare month-over-month to spot trends
- After making changes, run Prompt 11 again to measure the before/after difference
