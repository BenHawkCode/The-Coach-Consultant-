# Prompt 18: TikTok Trend Spotter

**Category:** Social Media Hacking
**Where to Use:** TikTok search results or discover page for your niche
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Analyses trending TikTok content visible on the page to identify formats, patterns, and trends worth jumping on. Extracts the structural elements that make content perform on the platform and shows you how to adapt those trends for your brand without looking like a copycat.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
You are looking at TikTok content related to my niche. Analyse every visible video and trend signal on this page.

# Trending Format Analysis
- What video formats appear most frequently (talking head, text overlay, voiceover, skit, duet, stitch, slideshow)?
- Which formats are getting the highest engagement based on visible metrics?
- What video lengths seem to dominate (short punchy vs longer form)?
- Are there any format patterns that appear to be trending right now?

# Hook Patterns
- List the first 2-3 seconds of every visible video (the text or spoken hook)
- What hook techniques are being used most (text on screen, direct question, bold statement, story start)?
- Which hooks have the highest view counts?
- Extract the top 5 hook formulas as reusable templates

# Audio & Sound Trends
- Are there trending sounds or audio clips being used across multiple videos?
- Are creators using original audio, trending sounds, or voiceovers?
- Which audio approach correlates with higher engagement?

# Content Theme Analysis
- What specific topics or angles are getting the most traction in [YOUR NICHE]?
- What pain points or desires are creators addressing?
- What tone dominates (educational, entertaining, controversial, motivational)?
- Are there any content angles that are oversaturated vs underserved?

# Engagement Patterns
- What's driving comments - controversy, questions, relatability, or something else?
- Are creators using specific engagement triggers (polls, questions, controversial takes)?
- What makes viewers share or save these videos?

# Trend Adaptation Plan
Based on what's visible, give me:
1. The top 3 trends I should jump on this week for [YOUR NICHE]
2. For each trend, a specific video concept adapted for [YOUR AUDIENCE]
3. The exact hook, format, and approximate length I should use
4. What to avoid - trends that are already dying or oversaturated

My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
My audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
My offer: [YOUR OFFER]
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Format breakdown showing which video styles are performing best right now
- Hook formula library extracted from top-performing TikTok content
- Audio and sound trend intelligence for your niche
- Content theme map showing what's oversaturated vs underserved
- 3 specific trend-jacking video concepts ready to film this week
- Clear guidance on what to avoid so you don't waste time on dying trends

## Pro Tips
- Run this weekly - TikTok trends move fast and what works changes every 7-14 days
- Search for niche-specific hashtags to get more relevant results than the generic discover page
- The formats that work on TikTok often translate directly to Instagram Reels - double your content output
