# Prompt 19: Facebook Group Intelligence

**Category:** Social Media Hacking
**Where to Use:** Any Facebook group page where your target audience hangs out
**ROI Level:** Medium | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Mines a Facebook group for raw audience intelligence - the exact language people use, the problems they're struggling with, the questions they keep asking, and the solutions they're already buying. This is market research gold that most business owners completely overlook.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
You are looking at a Facebook group relevant to my niche. Analyse every visible post, comment, and discussion to extract audience intelligence.

# Pain Point Extraction
- List every problem, frustration, or challenge mentioned in visible posts
- Rank them by frequency - which pain points come up most often?
- Note the EXACT language people use to describe their problems (not your interpretation, their actual words)
- Which pain points generate the most comments and engagement?

# Question Mining
- List every question being asked in this group
- Categorise them: beginner questions, intermediate, advanced
- Which questions get asked repeatedly (even in different words)?
- What knowledge gaps does this audience clearly have?

# Desire & Aspiration Mapping
- What outcomes or results are people trying to achieve?
- What does "success" look like to this audience based on what they discuss?
- What are they aspirational about vs what are they desperate about?

# Language & Vocabulary Bank
- Extract 15-20 specific phrases this audience uses to describe their situation
- What jargon, slang, or shorthand is common in this group?
- How do they talk about solutions vs how marketers typically talk about solutions?
- This is copy gold - I want their exact words, not polished versions

# Product & Service Intelligence
- What products, tools, services, or solutions are people recommending to each other?
- What are people spending money on in this space?
- What complaints do they have about existing solutions?
- Where are people saying "I wish there was something that..."?

# Content Ideas
Based on everything extracted, give me:
1. 10 content topics that would resonate strongly with this audience
2. 5 lead magnet ideas based on their most common questions
3. 3 offer angles based on their biggest pain points and desired outcomes

Frame everything for [YOUR NICHE] targeting [YOUR AUDIENCE] selling [YOUR OFFER].
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Ranked pain point list with exact audience language for each
- Question bank categorised by sophistication level
- Desire and aspiration map showing what drives this audience
- 15-20 phrase vocabulary bank for use in your copy and content
- Competitive intelligence on what products/services they're already buying
- 10 content topics, 5 lead magnet ideas, and 3 offer angles

## Pro Tips
- The language extraction is the most valuable part - use those exact phrases in your ads, emails, and sales pages
- Join 3-5 groups in your niche and run this on each one to spot universal patterns
- Sort by "New Activity" or "Top Posts" for different intelligence - recent posts show current problems, top posts show evergreen pain points
