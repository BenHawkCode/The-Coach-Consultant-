# Prompt 15: LinkedIn Profile Optimiser

**Category:** Social Media Hacking
**Where to Use:** Your own LinkedIn profile page
**ROI Level:** High | **Setup:** Easy

## What This Does
Runs a full LinkedIn profile audit covering every section that affects whether someone connects, follows, or reaches out. Analyses your headline, about section, featured content, experience framing, and overall positioning - then gives you specific rewrites and changes to increase inbound opportunities.

---

## Copy & Paste Prompt

```
You are looking at my LinkedIn profile. Run a complete profile optimisation audit on every visible section.

# Headline Analysis
- Rate my headline out of 10 for clarity and search visibility
- Does it clearly communicate who I help and what result I deliver?
- Rewrite my headline in 3 different variations optimised for [YOUR NICHE]

# Banner Image Assessment
- Does the banner reinforce my positioning and authority?
- What should the banner communicate if it's weak or missing?

# About Section Audit
- Rate the about section out of 10 for conversion potential
- Does it follow a strong structure (hook, problem, solution, proof, CTA)?
- Is it written for my target audience or is it self-indulgent?
- Rewrite the first 3 lines (the part visible before "see more") to maximise click-through

# Featured Section
- What's in the featured section and is it strategically chosen?
- What should be featured to drive the most conversions for [YOUR OFFER]?
- Suggest 3 ideal featured items with explanations

# Experience Section
- Is my experience framed as results and outcomes, or just job descriptions?
- What changes would make this section build more authority?

# Recommendations & Social Proof
- How strong is the social proof visible on the profile?
- What types of recommendations should I proactively request?

# Search Optimisation
- What keywords should appear throughout my profile to rank for [YOUR NICHE] searches?
- List 10 keywords I should naturally weave into my headline, about, and experience sections

# Profile Rewrite Priority List
Give me a ranked list of the top 5 changes that would have the biggest impact on inbound leads and connection requests. For each one, tell me exactly what to change and provide the new copy.

My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
My target audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
My main offer: [YOUR OFFER]
My website: [YOUR WEBSITE]
```

---

## What You'll Get Back
- Headline score with 3 optimised rewrite variations
- About section audit with a rewritten opening hook
- Featured section strategy with specific content recommendations
- Experience section reframing advice for authority building
- 10 target keywords for LinkedIn search optimisation
- Prioritised top 5 changes with exact copy to implement

## Pro Tips
- The first 3 lines of your about section do 80% of the work - nail those first
- Update your headline seasonally to test which version drives more profile views
- After making changes, check your LinkedIn dashboard weekly to measure search appearance and profile view increases
