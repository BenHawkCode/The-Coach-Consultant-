1. Talking Reel
2. BTS Reel
3. Voice-Over Reel
4. Carousel
5. Client Result

## Notes
- AM Posts: Reels on weekdays, Carousels on weekends
- PM Posts: Mix of Talking Reels, Carousels, Voice-Over Reels. Sunday is open/flexible
- Company Page: Daily at 12:00, mix of formats
- YouTube: Tuesday, Thursday, Sunday only at 18:00
- All content types must be selected from the 5 valid types listed above
