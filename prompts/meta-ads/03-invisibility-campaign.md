```
Read 1-meta-ads/meta-ad-copy/skill.md and follow the instructions to generate meta ad copy for business owners with amazing client results but completely invisible online. They rely 100% on referrals. Campaign goal: book a visibility call.
```

---

## Pre-filled Context

When skill asks questions, answer:

**Question 1: Campaign Goal**
→ Book a Call

**Question 2: Main Pain**
→ Invisibility

**Question 3: Core Offer**
→ Free 45-minute visibility call to build AI-powered content system that generates 20+ posts/week from existing client work

**Question 4: Include**
→ Select: Social Proof, Specific Metrics, Authority Signals

---

## Expected Output

3-5 variations targeting invisibility pain:
- "Brilliant results. Zero visibility. Sound familiar?"
- "100% referrals = feast or famine client flow"
- "1,200+ business owners went from invisible to visible. Free call → your system."

---

## When to Use

- Target audience: 5+ years experience, strong results, no marketing
- Pain point: Nobody knows they exist, sporadic posting, referral-dependent
- Transformation: 20+ posts/week, predictable visibility
