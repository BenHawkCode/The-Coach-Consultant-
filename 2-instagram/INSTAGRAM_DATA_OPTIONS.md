# Instagram Data Collection Options

Instagram has aggressive anti-scraping measures. Here are our options:

## ❌ What Doesn't Work
- **Instaloader**: 403 Forbidden (Instagram blocks it)
- **instagram-scraper**: Deprecated/not maintained
- **Direct scraping**: Instagram blocks bots

## ✅ What Works

### Option 1: Manual Export (RECOMMENDED - Fastest)
Ask Ben to export his Instagram Insights data directly from the app.

**Steps:**
1. Instagram app → Profile → Insights
2. Tap "Export Data" (top right)
3. Select last 90 days
4. Download CSV
5. Send to us

**Pros:**
- ✅ Official data (no scraping)
- ✅ Includes saves, shares, reach, impressions
- ✅ 100% accurate
- ✅ Takes 2 minutes

**Cons:**
- ❌ Requires Ben's action

---
